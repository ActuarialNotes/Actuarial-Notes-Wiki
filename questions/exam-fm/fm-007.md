A 10,000 par value 10-year bond with 8% annual coupons is bought at a premium to yield an annual effective rate of 6%.

Calculate the interest portion of the 7th coupon.

- A) 632
- B) 642
- C) 651
- D) 660
- E) 667

## Explanation

The book value at time 6 is the present value of all future payments (4 remaining coupons plus redemption):

$$BV_6 = 10{,}000v^4 + 800a_{\overline{4}|0.06} = 7920.94 + 2772.08 = 10{,}693$$

The interest portion of the 7th coupon is:

$$10{,}693 \times 0.06 = 641.58$$
