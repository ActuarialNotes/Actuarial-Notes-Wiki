Which of the following is NOT a non-level annuity?

- A) $500 at the end of each year for 10 years
- B) $100, $200, $300, … increasing each year
- C) Payments growing 4% every year
- D) $1,000 in year 1, nothing in year 2, then $3,000

<!-- rationale: 0 is the correct pick (it is level). 1: arithmetic non-level · 2: geometric non-level · 3: irregular non-level -->
