# Sample Space

## Definition

The ==sample space== is the set $S$ (or $\Omega$) of all possible outcomes of a random experiment.

$$ S = \{s_1, s_2, \ldots\} $$

> [!example]- <u>Example</u>
> **Rolling two dice:** The sample space has $6 \times 6 = 36$ outcomes:
> $$S = \{(1,1), (1,2), \ldots, (1,6), (2,1), \ldots, (6,6)\}$$
>
> **Flipping a coin three times:**
> $$S = \{HHH, HHT, HTH, HTT, THH, THT, TTH, TTT\}$$
> This sample space has $2^3 = 8$ outcomes.
