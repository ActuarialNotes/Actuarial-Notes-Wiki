Annual profit $\sim N(\mu,\mu^3)$. $P(\text{loss})=5\%$. Calculate expected profit.

- A) 370 million
- B) 520 million
- C) 780 million
- D) 950 million
- E) 1645 million

## Explanation

$\Phi(-1/\sqrt\mu)=0.05\Rightarrow1/\sqrt\mu=1.645\Rightarrow\mu=(1/1.645)^2\approx0.370$ billion $=\mathbf{370}$ million
