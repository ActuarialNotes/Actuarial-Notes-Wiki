Two deposits are made into a fund: 300 at time 0 and $X$ at time 4. The force of interest for the fund is $\delta_t = \dfrac{t}{50}$, $t \ge 0$. The amount of interest earned from time 0 to time 10 is $4X$.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 145
- B) 173
- C) 181
- D) 192
- E) 201

## Explanation

The accumulation function: $a(t) = \exp\!\left(\int_0^t \frac{r}{50}\,dr\right) = \exp\!\left(\frac{t^2}{100}\right)$.

Balance at time 10: $300\,a(10) + X\cdot\frac{a(10)}{a(4)} = 300e^{1} + X\,e^{1-0.16} = 815.48 + 2.31637X$

Total deposits: $300 + X$

Interest earned $= (815.48 + 2.31637X) - (300 + X) = 515.48 + 1.31637X = 4X$

$$515.48 = 2.68363X \implies X = 192.08$$
