A company's profit $Y$ satisfies: $P[Y > 0] = 0.75$, $P[Y > 100] = 0.50$, $P[Y > 200] = 0.25$.

Calculate $E[Y/100]$.

- A) 0.5
- B) 1.0
- C) 1.5
- D) 2.0
- E) 2.5

## Explanation

The equal spacing of the percentiles suggests $Y \sim \text{Uniform}[a, b]$.

$P(Y>100) = 0.50 \Rightarrow$ median $= 100$, so the mean of the uniform is 100.

$P(Y>0) = 0.75 \Rightarrow (b-0)/(b-a) = 0.75$. Combined with $P(Y>200)=0.25$:
solving gives $a = -100$, $b = 300$.

$$E[Y] = \frac{-100+300}{2} = 100$$

$$E[Y/100] = \frac{100}{100} = \mathbf{1.0}$$
