A residual-vs-fitted plot shows the residuals fanning out (spread widening) as the fitted values grow. What does this indicate?

- A) Non-constant error variance (heteroscedasticity)
- B) An excellent fit, since the residuals grow with the prediction
- C) That a predictor is missing from the model
- D) That the residuals are perfectly Normal
