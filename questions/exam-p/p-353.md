Ten fair coins are tossed. Let $X$ be the number of heads and $Y$ the number of tails.

Calculate $\text{Cov}(X, Y)$.

- A) $-5/2$
- B) $-5/4$
- C) $0$
- D) $5/4$
- E) $5/2$

## Explanation

Since $X + Y = 10$, we have $Y = 10 - X$.

$$\text{Cov}(X, Y) = \text{Cov}(X,\; 10-X) = -\text{Var}(X)$$

$X \sim \text{Binomial}(10, 0.5)$, so $\text{Var}(X) = 10(0.5)(0.5) = 5/2$.

$$\text{Cov}(X,Y) = -\frac{5}{2}$$
