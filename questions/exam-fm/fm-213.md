A six-year 1000 face amount bond has an annual coupon rate of 8% semiannually. The bond currently sells for 911.37.

Calculate the annual effective yield rate.

- A) 7.29%
- B) 8.00%
- C) 9.72%
- D) 10.00%
- E) 10.25%

## Explanation

Using a financial calculator: $n=12$, $PV=-911.37$, $PMT=40$, $FV=1000$.

$CPT\ I/Y = 5.0\%$ (semiannual), so $i^{(2)}/2 = 5\%$.

Annual effective rate: $i = (1.05)^2 - 1 = 0.1025 = 10.25\%$.
