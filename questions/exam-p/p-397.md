A random variable $T$ represents the remaining useful life of a machine and follows a uniform distribution on $[0, 5]$.

Calculate the expected remaining useful life, given that the machine has already survived the first 2 years (i.e., $T > 2$).

- A) 0.5
- B) 1.0
- C) 1.5
- D) 2.0
- E) 2.5

## Explanation

Given $T > 2$, by conditioning on a uniform distribution: $T \mid T > 2 \sim \text{Uniform}[2, 5]$.

$$E[T \mid T > 2] = \frac{2+5}{2} = 3.5$$

$$E[5-T \mid T > 2] = 5 - 3.5 = \mathbf{1.5}$$
