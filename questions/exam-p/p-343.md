Let $X$ represent the number of defective parts in a shipment of five parts.
$$P[X \geq x] = 1 - \left(1-\frac{1}{5}\right)^x = 1-\left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^x, \quad x=1,2,3,4,5$$

Calculate $E(X)$.

- A) 0.9
- B) 1.1
- C) 2.1
- D) 2.3
- E) 3.9

## Explanation

For a non-negative integer-valued random variable:

$$E[X] = \sum_{x=1}^{5} P[X \geq x] = \sum_{x=1}^{5}\!\left[1-\left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^x\right]$$

$$= 5 - \sum_{x=1}^{5}\left(\frac{4}{5}\right)^x = 5 - \frac{(4/5)[1-(4/5)^5]}{1-4/5}$$

$$= 5 - 4\!\left[1-\frac{1024}{3125}\right] = 5 - 4\!\cdot\!\frac{2101}{3125} = \frac{15625-8404}{3125} \approx \mathbf{2.31 \approx 2.3}$$
