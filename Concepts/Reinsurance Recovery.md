[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Reinsurance Recovery**

## Definition
==Reinsurance Recovery== is the portion of losses and LAE that will be recovered from reinsurers under reinsurance agreements, requiring separate estimation and development.

## Types
- Treaty reinsurance
- Facultative reinsurance
- Excess of loss
- Quota share
- Aggregate covers

## Calculation
```
Gross Losses = Total before reinsurance
Ceded Losses = Reinsurance recoveries
Net Losses = Gross - Ceded

Each component developed separately
```
