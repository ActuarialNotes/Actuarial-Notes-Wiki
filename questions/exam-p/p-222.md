Losses $\sim N(20000,4500^2)$, deductible 15000. Calculate the 95th percentile of losses exceeding the deductible.

- A) 27,400
- B) 27,700
- C) 28,100
- D) 28,400
- E) 28,800

## Explanation

$P(X\leq15000)\approx0.1333$. Setting $P(X\leq k)=0.05(0.1333)+0.95=0.9567$: $k=20000+4500(1.72)\approx\mathbf{27700}$
