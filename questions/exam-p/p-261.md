The probability that an agent sells an insurance policy to a potential customer during a first appointment is 0.20. The agent has scheduled first appointments with five potential customers. Calculate the probability that the agent sells an insurance policy during at least two of these appointments.

- A) 0.04
- B) 0.20
- C) 0.26
- D) 0.40
- E) 0.74

## Explanation

$X \sim \text{Binomial}(5, 0.20)$.

$$P(X=0) = 0.8^5 = 0.3277$$

$$P(X=1) = 5(0.2)(0.8^4) = 0.4096$$

$$P(X \geq 2) = 1 - 0.3277 - 0.4096 = \mathbf{0.2627 \approx 0.26}$$
