Policy pays 1000 for each severe weather event after the first two. $N\sim\text{Poisson}(1)$. Calculate the expected annual payment.

- A) 80
- B) 104
- C) 368
- D) 512
- E) 632

## Explanation

$E[(N-2)_+]=E[N]-E[\min(N,2)]=1-(2-3e^{-1})=3e^{-1}-1\approx0.1036$.

Expected payment $=1000\times0.1036\approx\mathbf{104}$
