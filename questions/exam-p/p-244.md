Joint probability table for accidents $N$ and hospitalizations $M$:
- $N=0$: $M=0$ (0.700)
- $N=1$: $M=0$ (0.150), $M=1$ (0.050)
- $N=2$: $M=0$ (0.060), $M=1$ (0.020), $M=2$ (0.010)
- $N=3$: $M=0$ (0.005), $M=1$ (0.002), $M=2$ (0.002), $M=3$ (0.001)

Calculate $E[M]$.

- A) 0.085
- B) 0.099
- C) 0.410
- D) 1.000
- E) 1.500

## Explanation

$E[M]=1(0.050+0.020+0.002)+2(0.010+0.002)+3(0.001)=0.072+0.024+0.003=\mathbf{0.099}$
