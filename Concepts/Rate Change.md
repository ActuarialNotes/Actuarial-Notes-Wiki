[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Rate Change**

## Definition
==Rate Change== is the percentage modification to insurance rates, either the indicated change from analysis or the implemented change after business judgment.

## Types

### Indicated Change
From actuarial analysis before adjustments

### Implemented Change
Actual change put into effect, may differ from indicated

### On-Level Factor
```
Adjustment for historical rate changes:
On-Level Factor = Current Rate / Historical Rate
```
