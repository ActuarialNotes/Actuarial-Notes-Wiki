Three fair dice are thrown.

Calculate the probability that the same number appears on exactly two of the three dice.

- A) 0.278
- B) 0.417
- C) 0.444
- D) 0.556
- E) 0.583

## Explanation

Number of outcomes where exactly 2 dice show the same value:

- Choose the repeated value: 6
- Choose which 2 dice form the pair: $\binom{3}{2}=3$
- Choose the different value for the 3rd die: 5

Total: $6 \times 3 \times 5 = 90$ out of $6^3=216$.

$$
\begin{align*}
P &= \frac{90}{216} \\
  &= \frac{5}{12} \\
  &\approx \mathbf{0.417}
\end{align*}
$$
