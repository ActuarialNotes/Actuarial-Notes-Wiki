Lifetime of a machine part $\sim\text{Exp}(\text{mean }5)$. Calculate the mean lifetime given that it survives less than ten years.

- A) 0.865
- B) 1.157
- C) 2.568
- D) 2.970
- E) 3.435

## Explanation

$E[T\cdot\mathbf{1}(T<10)]=5-15e^{-2}\approx2.970$, $P(T<10)=1-e^{-2}\approx0.8647$. Result: $2.970/0.8647\approx\mathbf{3.435}$
