$E[X^2]=61$, $E[(X-1)^2]=47$. Calculate the standard deviation of $X$.

- A) 2.18
- B) 2.40
- C) 7.31
- D) 7.50
- E) 7.81

## Explanation

$61-2E[X]+1=47\Rightarrow E[X]=7.5$. $\text{Var}(X)=61-56.25=4.75$. $\text{SD}=\sqrt{4.75}\approx\mathbf{2.18}$
