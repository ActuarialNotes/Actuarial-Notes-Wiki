Patients are tested for sleep apnea one at a time until the disease is found. Let $r=P(\text{at least 4 tested})$. Determine $P(\text{at least 12 tested}\mid\text{at least 4 tested})$.

- A) $r^{3/11}$
- B) $r^3$
- C) $r^{8/3}$
- D) $r^{1/2}$
- E) $r^{1/3}$

## Explanation

$r=P(X\geq4)=(1-p)^3$. By direct calculation:
$$P(X\geq12\mid X\geq4)=\frac{(1-p)^{11}}{(1-p)^3}=(1-p)^8=\left[(1-p)^3\right]^{8/3}=\mathbf{r^{8/3}}$$
