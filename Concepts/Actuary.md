## Actuary
An **Actuary** is a business professional who deals with the financial impact of [[risk]] and uncertainty. ([Wikidata](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q179985))

## What does an Actuary do?

> [!example]- In an uncertain world, an actuary... (SOA)
> ...anticipates future conditions and assesses the <u>risks</u> ahead. ([SOA](https://www.soa.org/future-actuaries/precandidate/what/))

>[!example]- Actuaries are problem solvers and strategic thinkers, who... (IFoA) 
>...use their mathematical skills to help measure the probability and <u>risk</u> of future events. ([IFoA](https://actuaries.org.uk/qualify/become-an-actuary/what-is-an-actuary/))

>[!example]- Actuaries assess the financial consequences of... (IRS)
>...<u>risks</u> and use mathematics, statistics and financial theory to analyze and determine the financial impact of uncertain future events. ([IRS](https://www.irs.gov/retirement-plans/what-is-an-actuary-a-brief-overview))
