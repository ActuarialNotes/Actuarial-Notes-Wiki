A group insurance policy covers 4 independent drivers, each with probability 0.10 of filing a claim in a given year. Calculate the expected number of claims filed in a year under this policy.

- A) 0.40
- B) 0.69
- C) 0.96
- D) 1.17
- E) 2.00

## Explanation

$N \sim \text{Binomial}(4, 0.10)$.

$$E[N] = np = 4 \times 0.10 = \mathbf{0.40}$$
