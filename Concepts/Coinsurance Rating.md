## Definition
==Coinsurance Rating== is the adjustment of rates when the policyholder shares in the loss through a coinsurance provision, similar to deductible rating.

## Formula
```
Coinsurance Rate = Base Rate × Coinsurance Factor

Where factor < 1.0 due to reduced insurer cost
```
