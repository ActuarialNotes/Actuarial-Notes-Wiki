Speed $V\sim\text{Uniform}[5,20]$. Given $V=v$, loss $L\sim\text{Exp}(\text{mean }3v)$. Calculate $\text{Var}(L)$.

- A) 525
- B) 1463
- C) 1575
- D) 1632
- E) 1744

## Explanation

$E[V]=12.5$, $E[V^2]=18.75+156.25=175$.

$E[\text{Var}(L|V)]=E[9V^2]=9\times175=1575$.

$\text{Var}(E[L|V])=\text{Var}(3V)=9\times18.75=168.75$.

$\text{Var}(L)=1575+168.75=\mathbf{1744}$
