A town averages 5 tornadoes in any four-year period; inter-arrival times are exponentially distributed and independent. Calculate the median number of years until the next tornado.

- A) 0.55
- B) 0.73
- C) 0.80
- D) 0.87
- E) 1.25

## Explanation

$\lambda=5/4$ per year. Median $=\ln2/\lambda=4\ln2/5\approx\mathbf{0.55}$
