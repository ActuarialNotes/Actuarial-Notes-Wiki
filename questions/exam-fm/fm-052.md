The prices of zero-coupon bonds are:

| Maturity | Price per 1 of face amount |
|---|---|
| 1 year | 0.95420 |
| 2 years | 0.90703 |
| 3 years | 0.85892 |

Calculate the one-year forward rate for year 3, ${}_1f_2$.

- A) 0.048
- B) 0.050
- C) 0.052
- D) 0.054
- E) 0.056

## Explanation

From the zero-coupon bond prices, extract spot rates:

$$
\begin{align*}
0.85892 &= \frac{1}{(1+s_3)^3} \implies s_3 \\
        &= 0.052
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
0.90703 &= \frac{1}{(1+s_2)^2} \implies s_2 \\
        &= 0.050
\end{align*}
$$

The forward rate satisfies $(1+s_3)^3 = (1+s_2)^2(1+{}_1f_2)$:

$$1.052^3 = 1.050^2(1+{}_1f_2)$$

$$
\begin{align*}
{}_1f_2 &= \frac{1.052^3}{1.050^2} - 1 \\
        &= 0.056
\end{align*}
$$
