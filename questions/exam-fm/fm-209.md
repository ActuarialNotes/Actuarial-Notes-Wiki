Sam deposits 5000 at the beginning of each year for ten years. The deposits earn an annual effective interest rate of $i$. All interest is reinvested at an annual effective interest rate of 5%.

Sam has 100,000 at the end of ten years.

Calculate $i$.

- A) 11.6%
- B) 15.6%
- C) 19.1%
- D) 23.2%
- E) 27.6%

## Explanation

The accumulated value of 10 deposits of 5000 (earning $i$ with reinvestment of interest at 5%):

$$5000(10) + 5000i(Is)_{\overline{10}|0.05} = 100,000$$

$$50,000 + 5000i\cdot\frac{\ddot{s}_{\overline{10}|0.05}-10}{0.05} = 100,000$$

$$5000i\cdot\frac{13.2068-10}{0.05} = 50,000$$

$$i\cdot\frac{3.2068}{0.05} = 10 \implies i = 10 \times 0.05/3.2068 = 0.1560 = 15.6\%$$
