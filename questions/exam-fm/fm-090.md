A 5-year loan of 500,000 with an annual effective discount rate of 8% is to be repaid by level end-of-year payments.

If the first four payments had been rounded up to the next multiple of 1,000, the final payment would be $X$.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 103,500
- B) 111,700
- C) 115,200
- D) 125,200
- E) 127,500

## Explanation

The annual effective interest rate is $i = 0.08/(1-0.08) = 0.08696$.

The level payment is $500{,}000/a_{\overline{5}|0.08696} = 500{,}000/3.9205 = 127{,}535$, which rounds up to 128,000.

The equation of value for the final payment $X$:
$$128{,}000 \cdot a_{\overline{4}|0.08696} + X(1.08696)^{-5} = 500{,}000$$
$$X = (500{,}000 - 417{,}466.36)(1.51729) = 125{,}227$$
