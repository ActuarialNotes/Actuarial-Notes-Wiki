[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Underwriting Year**

## Definition
==Underwriting Year== is a data organization method where policies are grouped by the year in which they were underwritten or written, regardless of their effective date.

## Characteristics
Similar to policy year but focuses on underwriting/binding date rather than effective date. Less commonly used than policy year or accident year in reserving.
