## Definition
==Stakeholder Reporting== is tailored communication of reserve results to different audiences, each with specific information needs and interests.

## Stakeholder Types
- Shareholders/investors
- Rating agencies
- Regulators
- Board of directors
- Senior management
- External auditors

## Customization by Audience
Each requires different:
- Level of detail
- Technical depth
- Focus areas
- Frequency
