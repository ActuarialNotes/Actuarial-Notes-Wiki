Start: 100 dollars. Each month $+1.10$ (prob 0.5) or $-0.90$ (prob 0.5), independent. Calculate $P(\text{value}>91\text{ after 100 months})$.

- A) 0.63
- B) 0.75
- C) 0.82
- D) 0.94
- E) 0.97

## Explanation

$E[X_i]=0.10$, $\text{Var}(X_i)=1.00$. $S=\sum X_i\approx N(10,100)$.

$P(100+S>91)=P(Z>-1.9)=\Phi(1.9)\approx\mathbf{0.97}$
