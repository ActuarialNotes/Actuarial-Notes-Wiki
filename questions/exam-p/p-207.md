Loss $L$ on an automobile claim has CDF
$$F(l)=\begin{cases}\frac{3l^3}{4V^3},&0\leq l<V\\1-\frac{1}{10}e^{-(l-V)/V},&l\geq V\end{cases}$$
Calculate $P(L>V)$.

- A) 0.00
- B) 0.10
- C) 0.25
- D) 0.75
- E) 0.90

## Explanation

$F(V)=1-\frac{1}{10}e^0=0.90$, so $P(L>V)=1-0.90=\mathbf{0.10}$
