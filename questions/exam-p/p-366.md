Let $X$ be a random variable with $P[X=0] = P[X=2] = 1/4$ and $P[X=1] = 1/2$.

Calculate $\text{Var}(2X+3)$.

- A) 0.5
- B) 1
- C) 2
- D) 4
- E) 8

## Explanation

$E[X] = 0(1/4)+1(1/2)+2(1/4) = 1$

$E[X^2] = 0+1(1/2)+4(1/4) = 1.5$

$\text{Var}(X) = 1.5-1 = 0.5$

$$\text{Var}(2X+3) = 4\,\text{Var}(X) = 4(0.5) = \mathbf{2}$$
