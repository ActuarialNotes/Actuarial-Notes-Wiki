The one-year forward rates, deferred $t$ years, are estimated to be:

| Year ($t$) | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Forward Rate | 4% | 6% | 8% | 10% | 12% |

Calculate the spot rate for a zero-coupon bond maturing three years from now.

- A) 4%
- B) 5%
- C) 6%
- D) 7%
- E) 8%

## Explanation

The spot rate $s_3$ for a 3-year zero-coupon bond is related to forward rates by:

$$s_1 = {}_1f_0 = 4\%$$

$$\begin{align*}
s_2 &= \sqrt{(1+{}_1f_0)(1+{}_1f_1)} - 1 \\
&= \sqrt{(1.04)(1.06)} - 1 \\
&= 4.995\%
\end{align*}$$

$$\begin{align*}
s_3 &= [(1+s_2)^2(1+{}_1f_2)]^{1/3} - 1 \\
&= [(1.04995)^2(1.08)]^{1/3} - 1 \\
&= 5.987\% \approx 6\%
\end{align*}$$
