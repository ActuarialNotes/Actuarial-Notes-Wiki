$M$ and $N$ are independent random variables with $E[M]=2$, $E[N]=8$, $E[M^2]=5$, $E[N^2]=68$.

Calculate $\text{Var}(MN)$.

- A) 4
- B) 8
- C) 16
- D) 60
- E) 84

## Explanation

Since $M$ and $N$ are independent, $E[(MN)^2] = E[M^2]E[N^2]$ and $E[MN] = E[M]E[N]$.

$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Var}(MN) &= E[M^2]E[N^2] - (E[M])^2(E[N])^2 \\
               &= 5 \times 68 - (2)^2(8)^2 \\
               &= 340-256 \\
               &= \mathbf{84}
\end{align*}
$$
