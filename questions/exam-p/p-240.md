1000 employees, death prob 0.014, independent. Death benefit 50,000 each. Find smallest fund (rounded to nearest 50,000) that covers benefits with probability $\geq0.99$.

- A) 750,000
- B) 850,000
- C) 1,050,000
- D) 1,150,000
- E) 1,400,000

## Explanation

$N\approx N(14,\sigma^2=13.80)$. 99th percentile: $14+2.326\times3.716\approx22.6\Rightarrow23$ deaths.

Fund $=23\times50000=\mathbf{1{,}150{,}000}$
