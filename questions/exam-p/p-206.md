Annual profit is normally distributed. $P(\leq 2000)=0.7642$, $P(\leq 3000)=0.9066$. Calculate $P(\leq 1000)$.

- A) 0.1424
- B) 0.3022
- C) 0.5478
- D) 0.6218
- E) 0.7257

## Explanation

$z=0.72$ at 2000 and $z=1.32$ at 3000 gives $\sigma=1667$, $\mu=800$. Then $P(\leq1000)=\Phi(0.12)\approx\mathbf{0.5478}$
