Four players enter a single-elimination tournament. Three are from Team A (each wins any given match with probability 1/2) and one is from Team B (wins any match with probability 1/3). The bracket is chosen randomly.

Calculate the probability that a player from Team A wins the tournament.

- A) $2/3$
- B) $3/4$
- C) $8/9$
- D) $5/9$
- E) $7/9$

## Explanation

In any bracket, Team B faces one Team A player in the semifinal.

$P(\text{B wins semifinal}) = 1/3$; $P(\text{B wins final against A}) = 1/3$.

$$
\begin{align*}
P(\text{B wins tournament}) &= \frac{1}{3} \times \frac{1}{3} \\
                            &= \frac{1}{9}
\end{align*}
$$

$$P(\text{A wins}) = 1 - \frac{1}{9} = \frac{8}{9}$$
