A random variable $X$ has density function
$$f(x) = \frac{3}{8}(x+1)^2, \quad -1 < x < 1$$

Calculate the 70th percentile of $X$.

- A) 0.50
- B) 0.62
- C) 0.78
- D) 0.88
- E) 0.94

## Explanation

$F(x) = \int_{-1}^x \frac{3}{8}(t+1)^2\,dt = \frac{(x+1)^3}{8}$.

Setting $F(p_{0.7}) = 0.70$:

$$(p_{0.7}+1)^3 = 5.6 \implies p_{0.7}+1 = 5.6^{1/3} \approx 1.776$$

$$p_{0.7} \approx 1.776-1 = \mathbf{0.776 \approx 0.78}$$
