Skateboarders A and B each attempt a stunt until injured; injury prob $=p$ per attempt, independent. $F(x,y)=P(A\leq x\text{ attempts},B\leq y\text{ attempts})$. Given $F(2,2)=0.0441$, calculate $F(1,5)$.

- A) 0.0093
- B) 0.0216
- C) 0.0495
- D) 0.0551
- E) 0.1112

## Explanation

$[1-(1-p)^2]^2=0.0441\Rightarrow(1-p)^2=0.79$.

$F(1,5)=p\cdot[1-(1-p)^5]=(1-\sqrt{0.79})(1-0.79^{2.5})=0.1112\times0.4454\approx\mathbf{0.0495}$
