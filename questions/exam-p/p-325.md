A company sponsors health insurance (H), life insurance (L), and retirement plans (R) for its employees. Each employee selects exactly two plans or none. Employee participation: 62.5% in H, 37.5% in L, 50% in R.

Calculate the percentage of employees who participate in both the life insurance and retirement plans.

- A) 12.5%
- B) 25.0%
- C) 37.5%
- D) 50.0%
- E) 62.5%

## Explanation

Each participant is in exactly 2 plans, so total participation-slots $= 62.5+37.5+50 = 150\%$. Since 2 slots per participant, the participation rate is 75%.

Let $x, y, z$ = percent in L∩R only, H∩R only, H∩L only respectively. Then:

$x+y+z = 75$ (total participants), $y+z = 62.5$, $x+z = 37.5$, $x+y = 50$.

Solving: $x = 75-62.5 = 12.5$, $y = 75-37.5 = 37.5$, $z = 75-50 = 25$.

L∩R participants $= x = \mathbf{12.5\%}$
