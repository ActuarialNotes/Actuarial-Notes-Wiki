[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Regulatory Reporting**

## Definition
==Regulatory Reporting== is communication of reserve estimates and supporting information to insurance regulators, following prescribed formats and standards.

## Requirements
- Annual statements
- Statement of Actuarial Opinion
- Actuarial Report
- Supporting documentation
- Compliance with ASOPs
