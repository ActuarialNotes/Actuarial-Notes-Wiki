An auto book has 500 claims over 5,000 car-years. Next year it writes 10,000 car-years but still has 500 claims. What happens to frequency?

- A) It halves, from 0.10 to 0.05
- B) It stays at 0.10, since the claim count is unchanged
- C) It doubles, from 0.10 to 0.20
- D) It rises to 500 claims per year
