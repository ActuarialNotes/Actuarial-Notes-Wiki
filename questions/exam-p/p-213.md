100 light bulbs, $P(\text{defective})=0.02$, independent. Calculate $P(\text{exactly 2 defective}\mid\leq2\text{ defective})$.

- A) 0.133
- B) 0.271
- C) 0.273
- D) 0.404
- E) 0.677

## Explanation

Using Poisson(2): $P(X=2)=2e^{-2}$, $P(X\leq2)=5e^{-2}$. Ratio $=2/5=\mathbf{0.40}$
