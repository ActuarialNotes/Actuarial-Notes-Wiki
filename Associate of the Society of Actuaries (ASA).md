# Associate of the Society of Actuaries (ASA)

The **Associate of the Society of Actuaries (ASA)** is a professional designation awarded by the [[Society of Actuaries (SOA)]] to candidates who have completed a defined set of exams, educational modules, and professional development requirements.

## Requirements

### Preliminary Exams
1. [[Exam P-1 (SOA)]] — Probability
2. [[Exam FM-2 (SOA)]] — Financial Mathematics
3. Exam FAM — Fundamentals of Actuarial Mathematics
4. Exam SRM — Statistics for Risk Modeling
5. Exam PA — Predictive Analytics

### Validation by Educational Experience (VEE)
- Economics
- Accounting and Finance

### Additional Requirements
- FAP (Fundamentals of Actuarial Practice) Modules
- APC (Associateship Professionalism Course)
