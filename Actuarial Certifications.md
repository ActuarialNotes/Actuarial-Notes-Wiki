# Actuarial Certifications

Actuarial certifications are professional credentials earned through a series of rigorous exams and educational requirements. The two primary credentialing bodies in North America are the Society of Actuaries (SOA) and the Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS).

## Society of Actuaries (SOA)

The [[Society of Actuaries (SOA)]] oversees credentialing for actuaries working in life insurance, health insurance, retirement benefits, and finance.

- [[Associate of the Society of Actuaries (ASA)]]

## Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS)

The CAS oversees credentialing for actuaries specializing in property and casualty insurance.
