Let $X$ and $Y$ be discrete random variables with joint probability function $p(x,y) = \frac{1}{x+1}\cdot\frac{1}{2^{x+1}}$ for $x = 0, 1, 2, \ldots$ and $y = 0, 1, \ldots, x$.

Calculate the variance of $Y$.

- A) 1.67
- B) 2.22
- C) 3.33
- D) 5.00
- E) 5.56

## Explanation

$P(X=x) = (x+1) \cdot \frac{1}{(x+1) \cdot 2^{x+1}} = \frac{1}{2^{x+1}}$.

Conditional on $X=x$, $Y$ is uniform on $\{0,1,\ldots,x\}$.

$$E[Y] = E[E[Y|X]] = E\!\left[\frac{X}{2}\right] = \frac{E[X]}{2} = \frac{1}{2}$$

(where $E[X] = \sum_{x=0}^\infty x/2^{x+1} = 1$)

$$E[Y^2] = E\!\left[\frac{X(2X+1)}{6}\right] = \frac{2E[X^2]+E[X]}{6}$$

With $E[X^2] = 3$: $E[Y^2] = \frac{6+1}{6} = \frac{7}{6}$.

$$\text{Var}(Y) = \frac{7}{6} - \left(\frac{1}{2}\right)^2 = \frac{7}{6} - \frac{1}{4} = \frac{14-3}{12} = \frac{11}{12} \approx 0.917$$

> **Note:** Based on the answer choices (all $>1$), there may be a discrepancy in how the joint PMF is specified. The official answer is B) 2.22.
