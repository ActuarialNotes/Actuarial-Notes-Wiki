A 1000 par value 20-year bond sells for $P$ and yields a nominal interest rate of 10% convertible semiannually. The bond has 9% coupons payable semiannually and a redemption value of 1200.

Calculate $P$.

- A) 914
- B) 943
- C) 1013
- D) 1034
- E) 1097

## Explanation

Semiannual coupon $= 1000(0.09/2) = 45$. Semiannual yield $= 5\%$. 40 periods.

$$P = 45a_{\overline{40}|0.05} + 1200(1.05)^{-40} = 45(17.1591) + 1200(0.14205) = 772.16 + 170.46 = 942.61$$
