$N\sim\text{Poisson}(1)$. Calculate $E[N\mid N\geq2]$.

- A) 0.63
- B) 2.39
- C) 2.54
- D) 3.00
- E) 3.78

## Explanation

$E[N\mid N\geq2]=\frac{1-e^{-1}}{1-2e^{-1}}=\frac{0.6321}{0.2642}\approx\mathbf{2.39}$
