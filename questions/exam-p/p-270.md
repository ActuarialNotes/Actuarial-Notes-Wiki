An industrial company provides health insurance to employees at four independent plants with the following statistics:

| Plant | Mean | SD |
|---|---|---|
| W | 2 | 1.0 |
| X | 2 | 1.0 |
| Y | 5 | 1.5 |
| Z | 7 | 2.0 |

Calculate the standard deviation of total company health insurance costs.

- A) 1.4
- B) 2.1
- C) 2.9
- D) 5.5
- E) 6.3

## Explanation

$$\text{Var}(\text{Total}) = 1.0^2 + 1.0^2 + 1.5^2 + 2.0^2$$

$$= 1 + 1 + 2.25 + 4 = 8.25$$

$$\text{SD} = \sqrt{8.25} \approx \mathbf{2.87 \approx 2.9}$$
