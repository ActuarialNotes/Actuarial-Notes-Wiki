Distribution A has mean $500 and SD $100; Distribution B has mean $2,000 and SD $300. Which has greater relative variability?

- A) A — its SD is a larger fraction of its mean (0.20 vs 0.15)
- B) B — it has the larger standard deviation (300 vs 100)
- C) B — it has the larger mean
- D) They are equal, since the measure ignores the mean
