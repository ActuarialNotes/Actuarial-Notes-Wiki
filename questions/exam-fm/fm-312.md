Deposits of 100 are made to an account today and one year from today. The annual force of interest at time $t$ in years for this account is:
$$\delta_t = 0.03 + 0.005t, \quad 0 \le t \le 3$$

Calculate the account balance two years from today.

- A) 211.07
- B) 211.87
- C) 216.05
- D) 216.79
- E) 220.24

## Explanation

The accumulation function: $a(t) = \exp\!\left(\int_0^t(0.03+0.005r)\,dr\right) = \exp(0.03t+0.0025t^2)$.

$a(2) = e^{0.06+0.01} = e^{0.07} = 1.0725$

$a(1) = e^{0.03+0.0025} = e^{0.0325} = 1.03304$

Balance at $t=2$:
$$100 \cdot a(2) + 100 \cdot \frac{a(2)}{a(1)} = 100(1.0725) + 100\cdot\frac{1.0725}{1.03304}$$

$$= 107.25 + 100(1.03818) = 107.25 + 103.82 = 211.07$$
