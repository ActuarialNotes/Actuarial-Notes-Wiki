# MCT Guideline 2026 (Effective January 1, 2026)

## Overview
Following a 90-day consultation launched May 2025 and final publication on November 20, 2025, OSFI's updated Minimum Capital Test guideline took effect for federally regulated P&C insurers, including foreign branches.

## Key Details
*   **Simplified Unexpired Coverage Formula:** The insurance-risk capital calculation for unexpired coverage was simplified for consistent interpretation, supported by a CIA educational note co-developed for appointed actuaries.
*   **Foreign Branch Clarifications:** Capital treatment of insurance receivables for branches narrowed to funds-withheld reinsurance arrangements, with OSFI's prior-review requirement removed.
*   **Administrative Updates:** Capital confirmation requirements for user fees clarified; part of OSFI's quarterly-release cadence that also rescinded dozens of legacy guidance documents.

## Multi-Agent Execution Mandates
*   **Actuarial & Finance:** Capital engines and the appointed actuary's unexpired-coverage calculations updated to the simplified formula for Q1 2026 filings onward.
*   **Risk:** Internal target ratios and ORSA projections re-run on the 2026 basis; branch entities revisit funds-withheld structures under the clarified treatment.
