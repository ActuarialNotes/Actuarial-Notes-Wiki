The present value of a perpetuity-immediate with a first payment of $P$ and successive annual increases of 9 at an annual effective interest rate of 6% is 2600.

Calculate $P$.

- A) 5.50
- B) 6.00
- C) 6.50
- D) 7.00
- E) 7.50

## Explanation

The perpetuity consists of a level component plus an arithmetically increasing component:

$$2600 = Pa_{\overline{\infty}|0.06} + 9v(Ia)_{\overline{\infty}|0.06} = \frac{P}{0.06} + \frac{9}{1.06}\cdot\frac{1.06}{0.06^2}$$

$$= \frac{P}{0.06} + \frac{9}{0.06^2} = \frac{P}{0.06} + 2500$$

$$\frac{P}{0.06} = 100 \implies P = 6$$
