## Exam FM-2 Syllabus

The **Financial Mathematics (FM-2) Exam** is a 3 hour exam with 30 multiple choice questions about financial mathematics concepts including the theory of interest and its application to annuities, bonds, loans, and other financial instruments.

### Learning Objectives

> [!example]- Time Value of Money {10-15%}
> ### Learning Objectives
> 1. Define and recognize the definitions of the following terms: interest rate, simple interest, compound interest, accumulation function, future value, current value, present value, net present value, discount factor, discount rate, convertible, force of interest.
> 2. Given any two of interest rate, present value, or future value, calculate the third based on simple or compound interest.

> [!example]- Annuities {15-20%}
> ### Learning Objectives
> 1. Define and recognize the definitions of the following terms: annuity-immediate, annuity-due, perpetuity, payable m-thly, level payment annuity, arithmetic increasing/decreasing annuity, geometric increasing/decreasing annuity, term of annuity.
> 2. Given an annuity with level or non-level payments, and given an interest rate, calculate the present value, future value, and accumulated value.

> [!example]- Loans {15-20%}
> ### Learning Objectives
> 1. Define and recognize the definitions of the following terms: principal, interest, term of loan, outstanding balance, final payment (balloon, drop), amortization.
> 2. Calculate the missing item, given any four of: the amount of a level payment, the ## of payments, the interest rate, the present value, the future value.

> [!example]- Bonds {15-20%}
> ### Learning Objectives
> 1. Define and recognize the definitions of the following terms: price, book value, amortization of premium, accumulation of discount, redemption value, par value/face value, yield rate, coupon, coupon rate, term of bond, callable/non-callable.
> 2. Given any four of price, redemption value, yield rate, coupon rate, or term of bond, calculate the fifth.

> [!example]- General Cash Flows and Portfolios {15-20%}
> ### Learning Objectives
> 1. Define and recognize the definitions of the following terms: yield rate/rate of return, dollar-weighted rate of return, time-weighted rate of return, current value, duration, convexity, portfolio, spot rate, forward rate, yield curve, stock price, stock dividend.
> 2. Calculate the current value of a set of cash flows.

> [!example]- Immunization {10-15%}
> ### Learning Objectives
> 1. Define and recognize the definitions of the following terms: cash-flow matching, immunization, Redington immunization.
> 2. Construct an investment portfolio to immunize a set of liability cash flows.
