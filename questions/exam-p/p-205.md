Time until next accident $\sim\text{Exp}(\text{mean }200)$. Calculate the probability of no accidents in the next 365 days, then at least one in the following 365 days.

- A) 0.026
- B) 0.135
- C) 0.161
- D) 0.704
- E) 0.839

## Explanation

$e^{-365/200}(1-e^{-365/200})\approx 0.161\times 0.839\approx\mathbf{0.135}$
