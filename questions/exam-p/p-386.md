A machine operates as long as at least 5 of its 8 components work. The probability that each component works is 0.90, and components work independently.

Calculate the probability that the machine operates.

- A) 0.813
- B) 0.900
- C) 0.950
- D) 0.970
- E) 0.995

## Explanation

$N \sim \text{Binomial}(8, 0.90)$. The machine operates when $N \geq 5$.

$$P(N \geq 5) = \sum_{k=5}^{8}\binom{8}{k}(0.9)^k(0.1)^{8-k}$$

$P(N=5) = 56(0.9^5)(0.1^3) = 0.0331$

$P(N=6) = 28(0.9^6)(0.1^2) = 0.1488$

$P(N=7) = 8(0.9^7)(0.1) = 0.3826$

$P(N=8) = (0.9^8) = 0.4305$

$$P(N \geq 5) = 0.0331+0.1488+0.3826+0.4305 = \mathbf{0.995}$$
