The present value of a perpetuity-due with payments of $X$ at the beginning of each 3-year period with an annual effective interest rate of 7% is 735.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 135
- B) 138
- C) 141
- D) 144
- E) 147

## Explanation

The 3-year effective interest rate is $1.07^3 - 1 = 0.225043$.

The perpetuity-due PV:
$$735 = X\ddot{a}_{\overline{\infty}|0.225043} = X\cdot\frac{1+0.225043}{0.225043} = X\cdot\frac{1.225043}{0.225043} = 5.443595X$$

$$X = 735 / 5.443595 = 135.02$$
