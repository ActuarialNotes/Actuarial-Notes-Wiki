**Linear Combinations of Random Variables** are expressions of the form $L = c_1 X_1 + c_2 X_2 + \cdots + c_n X_n$ for constants $c_i$, combining multiple random variables into a single quantity.
- Their moments are given by [[Moments for Linear Combinations]]
- Probabilities for normal linear combinations are given by [[Probabilities for Linear Combinations]]
- When $n$ is large and variables are i.i.d., the [[Central Limit Theorem]] applies
