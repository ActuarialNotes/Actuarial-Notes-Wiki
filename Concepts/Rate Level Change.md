[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Rate Level Change**

## Definition
==Rate Level Change== is the impact of historical rate modifications on loss costs and premium, requiring adjustment in reserving to ensure proper relationship between losses and exposures.

## Impact on Reserving
```
Rate changes affect:
- Reported loss amounts (limits may change)
- Mix of business (price sensitivity)
- Loss costs (anti-selection)
```

## Adjustments Needed
- On-level historical premium
- Adjust for exposure shifts
- Consider anti-selection effects
- Normalize for trending
