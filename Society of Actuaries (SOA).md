# Society of Actuaries (SOA)

The **Society of Actuaries** is the world's largest actuarial professional organization, serving over 32,000 members. The SOA administers exams and credentials for actuaries working in life insurance, health insurance, retirement benefits, investment, and enterprise risk management.
