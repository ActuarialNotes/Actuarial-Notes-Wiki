[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Policy Provision Changes**

## Definition
==Policy Provision Changes== refer to modifications in policy terms, conditions, exclusions, or coverage definitions that affect claim costs and patterns.

## Examples
- Deductible changes
- Limit modifications
- New exclusions
- Coverage expansions
- Changed definitions
- Different sub-limits

## Impact
- Ultimate costs affected
- Development patterns change
- Historical data needs adjustment
- May require pro-forma adjustments
