## What is an AI Agent?
An **Intelligent Agent** perceives its environment, takes actions autonomously in order to achieve goals, and may improve its performance with learning or acquiring knowledge.
- [Wikidata](https://www.wikidata.org/wiki/Q1142726)

## What is an Actuarial AI Agent?
An **Actuarial AI Agent** is an artificial intelligence system designed to perform or support actuarial tasks such as insurance pricing, reserving, and risk management.
