Two unbiased estimators of μ: the sample mean has variance σ²/n, the sample median about 1.57σ²/n. Which is more efficient, and why?

- A) The sample mean — for the same n it has the smaller variance
- B) The sample median — it is less affected by the tails
- C) They are equally efficient because both are unbiased
- D) Whichever one is faster to compute
