A purchase of 5020 is paid off with a loan at an annual effective interest rate of 6.25%. Payments are made at the end of each year until the loan is paid off. Each payment is 360 except for a final balloon payment, which is less than 720.

Calculate the amount of the balloon payment.

- A) 611
- B) 630
- C) 649
- D) 667
- E) 685

## Explanation

$5020 = 360a_{\overline{n}|0.0625} \implies n = 33.85$. Use $n=33$ full payments.

$5020 = 360a_{\overline{33}|0.0625} + Xv^{34}$

$5020 = 4980.95 + X(1.0625)^{-34} \implies 39.05 = X(0.13530) \implies X = 288.69$... but $B = 360 + 288.69 = 648.69 \approx 649$

(The 34th payment = regular 360 + remaining $288.75 = 648.75 \approx 649$.)
