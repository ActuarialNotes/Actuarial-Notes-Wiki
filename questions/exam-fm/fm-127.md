A loan of 20,000 is repaid by a payment of $X$ at the end of each year for 10 years. The loan has an annual effective interest rate of 11% for the first five years and 12% thereafter.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 2739.5
- B) 3078.5
- C) 3427.5
- D) 3467.5
- E) 3484.5

## Explanation

The level payment $X$ must satisfy the equation of value today:

$$
\begin{align*}
20,000 &= X\left(a_{\overline{5}|0.11} + 1.11^{-5} a_{\overline{5}|0.12}\right) \\
&= X\!\left(3.69590 + \frac{3.60478}{1.68506}\right) \\
&= X \cdot 5.83516 \\
X &= 20,000 / 5.83516 \\
  &= 3,427.50
\end{align*}
$$
