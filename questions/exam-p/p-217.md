Company pays death benefit 100/employee; insurance covers total over 400. $N\sim\text{Poisson}(2)$. Calculate expected company cost, excluding insurance.

- A) 171
- B) 189
- C) 192
- D) 200
- E) 208

## Explanation

Company pays $100\min(N,4)$. $E[\min(N,4)]=\sum_{k=0}^3P(N>k)\approx1.925$. Cost $\approx\mathbf{192}$
