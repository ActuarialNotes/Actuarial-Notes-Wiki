The sample variance S² = Σ(Xᵢ − x̄)² / (n − 1) divides by n − 1 rather than n specifically because:

- A) dividing by n − 1 makes E[S²] equal σ², removing the bias
- B) dividing by n − 1 gives the smallest possible variance
- C) n − 1 is the count after discarding one outlier
- D) it makes S² converge to σ² as n → ∞
