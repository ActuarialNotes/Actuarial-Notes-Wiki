Kevin makes a deposit at the end of each year for 10 years into a fund earning interest at a 4% annual effective rate. The first deposit is equal to $X$, with each subsequent deposit 9.2% greater than the previous year's deposit. The accumulated value of the fund immediately after the 10th deposit is 5000.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 255
- B) 260
- C) 270
- D) 279
- E) 293

## Explanation

The accumulated value of a geometrically increasing annuity-immediate with growth rate $g = 9.2\%$ and interest rate $i = 4\%$ uses the formula:

$$AV = X \cdot \frac{(1.092)^{10} - (1.04)^{10}}{1.092 - 1.04}$$

Setting $AV = 5000$:

$$
\begin{align*}
5000 &= X \cdot \frac{(1.092)^{10} - (1.04)^{10}}{0.092 - 0.04} \\
5000 &= X \cdot \frac{2.40822 - 1.48024}{0.052} \\
5000 &= X \cdot \frac{0.92798}{0.052} \\
5000 &= X \cdot 17.846
\end{align*}
$$

Equivalently, discounting to $PV = 5000/(1.04)^{10} = 3{,}377.82$ and dividing by the PV annuity factor:

$$X = 3{,}377.82 / 12.094 = 279.3$$
