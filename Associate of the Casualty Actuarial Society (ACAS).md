# Associate of the Casualty Actuarial Society (ACAS)

The **Associate of the Casualty Actuarial Society (ACAS)** is a professional designation awarded by the Casualty Actuarial Society (CAS) to candidates specializing in property and casualty insurance.

## Requirements

### Preliminary Exams (Shared with SOA)
1. [[Exam P-1 (SOA)]] — Probability
2. [[Exam FM-2 (SOA)]] — Financial Mathematics

### CAS-Specific Exams
3. Exam MAS-I — Modern Actuarial Statistics I
4. Exam MAS-II — Modern Actuarial Statistics II
5. Exam 5 — Basic Techniques for Ratemaking and Estimating Claim Liabilities
6. Exam 6 — Regulation and Financial Reporting

### Additional Requirements
- VEE (Validation by Educational Experience) credits
- Online courses in professionalism and insurance
