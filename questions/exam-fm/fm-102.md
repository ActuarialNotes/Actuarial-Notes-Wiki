Rhonda purchases a perpetuity providing a payment of 1 at the beginning of each year. The perpetuity's Macaulay duration is 30 years.

Calculate the modified duration of this perpetuity.

- A) 28.97
- B) 29.00
- C) 29.03
- D) 29.07
- E) 29.10

## Explanation

For a perpetuity-due, the Macaulay duration is $\ddot{a}_{\overline{\infty}|}/\text{PV}$ weighted by time. We have:

$$30 = MacD = \frac{Ia_{\overline{\infty}|}}{\ddot{a}_{\overline{\infty}|}} = \frac{1/(di)}{1/d} = \frac{1}{i}$$

So $i = 1/30$. The modified duration is:

$$ModD = \frac{MacD}{1+i} = \frac{30}{1+\frac{1}{30}} = \frac{30 \cdot 30}{31} = 29.032$$
