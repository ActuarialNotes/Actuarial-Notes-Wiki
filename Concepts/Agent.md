> An **agent** is an entity that acts or exerts power to produce an effect. 

> An **intelligent agent** is an agent that percieves its environment, take actions autonomously in order to achieve goal, and may improve its performance with [[Learning]] or acquiring [[Knowledge]].

## Agent-Based Model
