[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Net Losses**

## Definition
==Net Losses== are losses after deducting reinsurance and other recoveries, representing the amount the insurer will ultimately pay.

## Formula
```
Net = Gross - Ceded - Salvage & Subrogation - Other Recoveries
```
