Let $X \sim N(10, \sigma^2)$. It is given that $P[X > 20] = 0.05$.

Calculate $P[X > 5]$.

- A) 0.68
- B) 0.79
- C) 0.84
- D) 0.90
- E) 0.95

## Explanation

$P(X > 20) = 0.05 \Rightarrow \dfrac{20-10}{\sigma} = \Phi^{-1}(0.95) = 1.645$

$$\sigma = \frac{10}{1.645} \approx 6.08$$

$$P(X > 5) = P\!\left(Z > \frac{5-10}{6.08}\right) = P(Z > -0.822)$$

$$= \Phi(0.822) \approx \mathbf{0.794 \approx 0.79}$$
