Payments are made to an account at a continuous rate of $100e^{0.5t}$ from time $t = 1$ to time $t = 3$. The force of interest for this account is $\delta = 8\%$.

Calculate the value of the account at time $t = 4$.

- A) 313
- B) 432
- C) 477
- D) 606
- E) 657

## Explanation

The accumulated value at time 4 (accumulating from time $t$ to $t=4$ using force $\delta=0.08$):

$$\int_1^3 100e^{0.5t} \cdot e^{0.08(4-t)}\,dt = 100e^{0.32}\int_1^3 e^{0.5t}e^{-0.08t}\,dt = 100e^{0.32}\int_1^3 e^{0.42t}\,dt$$

$$= 100e^{0.32}\cdot\frac{e^{0.42t}}{0.42}\Bigg|_1^3 = 100e^{0.32}\cdot\frac{e^{1.26}-e^{0.42}}{0.42}$$

$$= 100e^{0.32}\cdot\frac{3.5254-1.5220}{0.42} = 100(1.3771)\cdot\frac{2.0034}{0.42} = 100(1.3771)(4.7700) = 656.8 \approx 657$$
