A marginal model plot overlays two curves against a predictor. What are the two curves, and what does a large gap between them mean?

- A) A smooth of the raw data vs the model's fitted smooth; a large gap signals model misspecification
- B) Two residual curves; a large gap means the residuals are Normal
- C) Upper and lower confidence bands; a gap means wide uncertainty
- D) The data smooth vs the model smooth; close agreement signals a poor fit
