$X\sim\text{Exp}(\text{mean }8)$. $Z=1$ with prob 0.45, $Z=0$ with prob 0.55. $X$ and $Z$ are independent. Calculate $\text{Var}(ZX)$.

- A) 13.0
- B) 15.8
- C) 28.8
- D) 35.2
- E) 44.6

## Explanation

$E[Z]=0.45$, $E[Z^2]=0.45$, $E[X]=8$, $E[X^2]=128$.

$E[ZX]=0.45\times8=3.6$, $E[(ZX)^2]=0.45\times128=57.6$.

$\text{Var}(ZX)=57.6-3.6^2=57.6-12.96=\mathbf{44.6}$
