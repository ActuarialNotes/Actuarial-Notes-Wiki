A liability consists of a series of 15 annual payments of 35,000 with the first payment due one year from now. The assets available to match this liability are 5-year and 10-year zero-coupon bonds. The annual effective interest rate is 6.2%.

Calculate the amount of the 5-year bond that should be purchased to achieve Redington immunization.

- A) 127,000
- B) 167,800
- C) 208,600
- D) 247,900
- E) 292,800

## Explanation

The present value of the liability is $PV = 35{,}000a_{\overline{15}|6.2\%} = 335{,}530.30$.

The Macaulay duration of the liability is:

$$\bar{d} = \frac{35{,}000v + 2(35{,}000)v^2 + \cdots + 15(35{,}000)v^{15}}{335{,}530.30} = \frac{2{,}312{,}521.95}{335{,}530.30} = 6.89214$$

Let $X$ = amount invested in the 5-year bond. For duration matching:

$$\frac{X}{335{,}530.30}(5) + \left(1 - \frac{X}{335{,}530.30}\right)(10) = 6.89214$$

$$X = 208{,}556$$
