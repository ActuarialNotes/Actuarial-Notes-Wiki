[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Claims Processing Changes**

## Definition
==Claims Processing Changes== refer to modifications in how claims are handled, investigated, adjusted, or settled, affecting the timing and pattern of loss emergence and development.

## Types of Changes
- New claims management system
- Revised settlement procedures
- Changed authority levels
- Staffing changes
- Outsourcing arrangements
- Process improvements

## Impact
- Settlement rates may change
- Development patterns shift
- Case reserve practices affected
- Historical data less relevant
