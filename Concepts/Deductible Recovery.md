[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Deductible Recovery**

## Definition
==Deductible Recovery== is the portion of claim costs reimbursed by policyholders under deductible provisions, reducing the insurer's net paid losses.

## Treatment
Similar to salvage and subrogation - develops separately from gross losses
