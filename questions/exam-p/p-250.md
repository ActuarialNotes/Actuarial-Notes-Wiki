A delivery service has two cars consuming 15 and 30 miles per gallon. Fuel costs 3 per gallon. Each car travels $D\sim N(25,3^2)$ miles daily, independent.

Calculate the probability that the total daily fuel cost is less than 7.

- A) 0.13
- B) 0.23
- C) 0.29
- D) 0.38
- E) 0.47

## Explanation

Total cost $C=3D_1/15+3D_2/30=D_1/5+D_2/10$.

$E[C]=25/5+25/10=5+2.5=7.5$.

$\text{Var}(C)=(1/5)^2(9)+(1/10)^2(9)=9/25+9/100=45/100=0.45$.

$$
\begin{align*}
P(C<7) &= P\!\left(Z<\frac{7-7.5}{\sqrt{0.45}}\right) \\
       &= \Phi(-0.745) \\
       &\approx \mathbf{0.228\approx0.23}
\end{align*}
$$
