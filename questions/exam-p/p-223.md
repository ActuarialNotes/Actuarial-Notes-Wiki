Monthly demand $\sim N(20,2^2)$. Stock level such that $P(\text{demand}>I)=0.02$. Calculate $I$.

- A) 16
- B) 23
- C) 24
- D) 32
- E) 43

## Explanation

$I=20+2\times\Phi^{-1}(0.98)=20+2(2.054)\approx\mathbf{24}$
