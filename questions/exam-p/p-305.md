A company administers a typing test to screen applicants. To pass, an applicant must complete the test in 50 minutes with no more than one error. The number of errors follows a Poisson distribution with mean 3. The time to complete the test follows a Normal distribution with mean 45 and standard deviation 10. The number of errors and the time required are independent.

Calculate the probability that a random applicant passes the test.

- A) 0.10
- B) 0.14
- C) 0.19
- D) 0.84
- E) 0.89

## Explanation

$P(\leq 1 \text{ error}) = e^{-3}(1+3) = 4e^{-3} \approx 0.1991$

$P(\text{time} \leq 50) = \Phi\!\left(\frac{50-45}{10}\right) = \Phi(0.5) \approx 0.6915$

$$P(\text{pass}) = 0.1991 \times 0.6915 \approx \mathbf{0.138 \approx 0.14}$$
