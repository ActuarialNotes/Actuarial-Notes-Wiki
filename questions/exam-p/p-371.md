A random variable $N$ has $E[N]=2$ and $\text{Var}(N)=6$. Conditional on $N=n$, $X$ follows a Poisson distribution with mean $n$.

Calculate $E[X^2]$.

- A) 8
- B) 10
- C) 12
- D) 14
- E) 16

## Explanation

$E[X|N=n] = n$ (Poisson mean).

$E[X^2|N=n] = \text{Var}(X|N)+[E[X|N]]^2 = n+n^2$.

$$
\begin{align*}
E[X^2] &= E[N+N^2] \\
       &= E[N]+E[N^2] \\
       &= E[N]+\text{Var}(N)+E[N]^2 \\
       &= 2+6+4 \\
       &= \mathbf{12}
\end{align*}
$$
