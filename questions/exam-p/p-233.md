Box: 2 diamonds, 3 spades, 5 hearts. Draw 2 without replacement. Calculate $\text{Var}(\text{diamonds}\mid\text{no spade drawn})$.

- A) 0.24
- B) 0.28
- C) 0.32
- D) 0.34
- E) 0.41

## Explanation

Given no spade: draw from 7 cards (2 diamonds, 5 hearts). Hypergeometric($N=7,K=2,n=2$):
$$
\begin{align*}
\text{Var} &= 2\cdot\tfrac{2}{7}\cdot\tfrac{5}{7}\cdot\tfrac{5}{6} \\
           &= \tfrac{100}{294} \\
           &\approx \mathbf{0.34}
\end{align*}
$$
