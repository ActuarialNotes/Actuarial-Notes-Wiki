A loan of 10,000 is repaid with a payment made at the end of each year for 20 years. The payments are 100, 200, 300, 400, and 500 in years 1 through 5, respectively. In the subsequent 15 years, equal annual payments of $X$ are made. The annual effective interest rate is 5%.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 842
- B) 977
- C) 1017
- D) 1029
- E) 1075

## Explanation

The first 5 payments form an increasing annuity. The PV of all payments equals the loan:

$$10,000 = 100(Ia)_{\overline{5}|} + Xv^5a_{\overline{15}|}$$

$$
\begin{align*}
100(Ia)_{\overline{5}|} &= 100\cdot\frac{\ddot{a}_{\overline{5}|} - 5v^5}{0.05} \\
                        &= 1{,}256.64
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
10{,}000 &= 1{,}256.64 + Xv^5a_{\overline{15}|} \\
&= 1{,}256.64 + X(0.78353)(10.3797)
\end{align*}
$$

$$
\begin{align*}
X &= \frac{8{,}743.36}{8.13273} \\
  &= 1075
\end{align*}
$$
