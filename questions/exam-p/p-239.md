$F(x,y)=[1-(0.5)^{x+1}][1-(0.2)^{y+1}]$ for nonneg integers. Calculate $P(X=3,Y=3)$.

- A) 0.00004
- B) 0.00040
- C) 0.03244
- D) 0.06800
- E) 0.12440

## Explanation

$P(X=3)=(0.5)^4=1/16$, $P(Y=3)=0.8\times(0.2)^3=0.0064$.

$P(X=3,Y=3)=(1/16)(0.0064)=\mathbf{0.0004}$
