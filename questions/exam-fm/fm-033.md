Seth borrows $X$ for four years at an annual effective interest rate of 8%, to be repaid with equal payments at the end of each year. The outstanding loan balance at the end of the third year is 559.12.

Calculate the principal repaid in the first payment.

- A) 444
- B) 454
- C) 464
- D) 474
- E) 484

## Explanation

With one payment remaining, the payment amount is $559.12(1.08) = 603.85$.

The amount borrowed is $603.85\,a_{\overline{4}|0.08} = 2000$.

Interest in the first payment $= 2000(0.08) = 160$, so principal repaid $= 603.85 - 160 = 443.85$.

**Alternatively**, principal repayments form a geometrically increasing sequence with ratio $(1.08)$. The final principal repaid equals the outstanding balance before that payment, i.e., 559.12. Working back three periods:

$$\text{Principal}_1 = \frac{559.12}{1.08^3} = 443.85$$
