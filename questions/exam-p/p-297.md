The lifetime of a machine part has a continuous distribution on the interval $(0, 40)$ with probability density function $f$, where $f(x)$ is proportional to $(10+x)^{-2}$ on the interval.

Calculate the probability that the lifetime of the machine part is less than 5.

- A) 0.03
- B) 0.13
- C) 0.42
- D) 0.58
- E) 0.97

## Explanation

The normalizing constant is found from $\int_0^{40}(10+x)^{-2}dx = 1/10-1/50 = 2/25$.

So $f(x) = \frac{25}{2}(10+x)^{-2}$.

$$
\begin{align*}
F(5) &= \frac{25}{2}\int_0^5(10+x)^{-2}dx \\
     &= \frac{25}{2}\left[\frac{1}{10}-\frac{1}{15}\right] \\
     &= \frac{25}{2} \cdot \frac{1}{30} \\
     &= \frac{25}{60} \\
     &= \frac{5}{12} \\
     &\approx \mathbf{0.417 \approx 0.42}
\end{align*}
$$
