A bag contains 3 red marbles and 5 blue marbles. Two marbles are drawn without replacement. What is the probability that both marbles drawn are red?

- A) 9/64
- B) 6/64
- C) 3/28
- D) 1/8

## Explanation

Drawing without replacement, use the multiplication rule for dependent events:

$$P(\text{both red}) = P(\text{1st red}) \times P(\text{2nd red} \mid \text{1st red}) = \frac{3}{8} \times \frac{2}{7} = \frac{6}{56} = \frac{3}{28}$$
