A level annuity pays $1,000 at the end of each year for 3 years at 5%. Which expression gives its present value?

- A) 1000 × (1 − 1.05⁻³)/0.05
- B) 1000 × 3, the sum of the payments
- C) 1000 × 1.05⁻³
- D) 1000 × (1.05³ − 1)/0.05
