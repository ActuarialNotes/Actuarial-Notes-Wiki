The number of years until a policyholder files a claim follows a geometric distribution with success probability $p = 1/4$.

Calculate the probability that the policyholder files a claim after year 5, given that no claim has been filed in the first 3 years.

- A) $9/16$\n- B) $3/4$\n- C) $9/64$\n- D) $1/2$\n- E) $1/4$

## Explanation

By the **memoryless property** of the geometric distribution:

$$P(X>5 \mid X>3) = P(X>2) = (1-p)^2 = \left(\frac{3}{4}\right)^2 = \frac{9}{16}$$
