A 1000 par value ten-year bond with 7.6% semiannual coupons is bought to yield 6.0% convertible semiannually.

Calculate the book value of the bond at the end of year six immediately after the coupon payment.

- A) 1054
- B) 1056
- C) 1059
- D) 1062
- E) 1065

## Explanation

Semiannual coupon $= 38$, yield $j = 3\%$. Book value at end of year 6 (= after 12th coupon, 8 periods remaining):

$$BV_{12} = 38a_{\overline{8}|0.03} + 1000(1.03)^{-8} = 38(7.01969) + 1000(0.78941) = 266.75 + 789.41 = 1056.16$$
