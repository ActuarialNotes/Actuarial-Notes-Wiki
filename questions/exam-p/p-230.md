Losses $\sim\text{Uniform}[0,b]$, deductible $b/2$. Calculate the ratio of $\text{Var}(\text{claim payment})$ to $\text{Var}(\text{loss})$.

- A) 1:8
- B) 3:16
- C) 1:4
- D) 5:16
- E) 1:2

## Explanation

$\text{Var}(Y)=5b^2/192$, $\text{Var}(X)=b^2/12$. Ratio $=5b^2/192\div b^2/12=60/192=\mathbf{5:16}$
