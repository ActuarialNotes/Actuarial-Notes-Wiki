A loan of $X$ is repaid with level payments of $R$ payable at the end of each year for $n$ years.

You are given:

  i) The interest paid in year 1 is 797.50.
  ii) The principal repaid in year $n - 4$ is 865.
  iii) The principal outstanding at the end of year $n - 1$ is 1144.50.

Determine $X$.

- A) 9,500
- B) 10,000
- C) 10,500
- D) 11,000
- E) 11,500

## Explanation

The principal outstanding at end of year $n-1$ equals $R \cdot a_{\overline{1}|} = Rv$. Also, it's the last payment discounted: $OB_{n-1} = Rv = 1144.50$, so $R = 1144.50(1+i)$.

Principal repaid in year $n-4$: $Rv^{n-(n-4)+1} = Rv^5 = 865$.

Dividing: $Rv^5 / Rv = v^4 = 865/1144.50 = 0.75579 \implies v = 0.93240 \implies i = 7.251\%$.

From (iii): $R = 1144.50/v = 1144.50(1.07251) = 1227.44$.

From (i): interest in year 1 $= Xi = 797.50 \implies X = 797.50/0.07251 = 10,999 \approx 11,000$.
