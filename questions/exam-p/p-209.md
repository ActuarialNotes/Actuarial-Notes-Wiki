Let $X$ have density $f(x)=2xe^{-x^2}$ for $x>0$. Calculate $P[0.5\leq X\leq 1.0]$.

- A) 0.433
- B) 0.547
- C) 0.632
- D) 0.731
- E) 0.865

## Explanation

CDF: $F(x)=1-e^{-x^2}$. $P(0.5\leq X\leq1)=e^{-0.25}-e^{-1}=0.7788-0.3679\approx0.411$.

> **Note:** The computed value is approximately 0.411; the official answer A) 0.433 may reflect a different parameterization in the original source.
