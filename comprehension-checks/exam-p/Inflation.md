Losses grow 10% due to inflation, but the policy's deductible stays fixed. How does the insurer's expected payment change?

- A) It rises by more than 10%, since the fixed deductible covers a smaller share of the larger losses
- B) It rises by exactly 10%, matching the inflation rate
- C) It rises by less than 10%, because the deductible absorbs the increase
- D) It stays the same, since the deductible is unchanged
