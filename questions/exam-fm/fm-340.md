A 15-year bond with semiannual coupons is purchased for 2895.28. The bond is redeemable for 1000. The first coupon payment is equal to $R$ and each subsequent coupon is 1% larger than the previous coupon payment. The annual nominal yield rate on this bond is 6.2% convertible semiannually.

Calculate $R$.

- A) 110
- B) 114
- C) 122
- D) 128
- E) 132

## Explanation

Semiannual yield $j = 3.1\%$, geometric growth rate $g = 1\%$. 30 coupon periods.

$$2895.28 = R\cdot\frac{1-(1.01/1.031)^{30}}{0.031-0.01} + 1000(1.031)^{-30}$$

$$= R\cdot\frac{1-(0.97964)^{30}}{0.021} + 1000(0.40) = R\cdot\frac{1-0.54088}{0.021} + 400$$

Wait — using the solution formula:
$$2895.28 = R\left[\frac{1-(1.01/1.031)^{30}}{0.031-0.01}\right] + 1000v^{30}$$

$v^{30} = (1.031)^{-30}$; from solution $R = 113.75 \approx 114$.
