Among all unbiased estimators of θ, the UMVUE has the smallest variance. Which statement is correct?

- A) Its variance is still bounded below by the Cramér–Rao bound 1/I(θ)
- B) It has zero variance by construction
- C) It is guaranteed to equal the MLE
- D) Reducing its bias further would lower its variance

<!-- rationale: 1: an estimator cannot have zero variance by construction · 2: the MLE is not generally the UMVUE in finite samples · 3: an unbiased estimator's bias is already zero, so there is none left to reduce -->
