A car and a bus arrive uniformly in $[7{:}15,7{:}30]$, independently. A train halts traffic from 7:20 to 7:25. Calculate $P(\text{waiting time of car or bus}>3\text{ min})$.

- A) 0.25
- B) 0.27
- C) 0.36
- D) 0.40
- E) 0.56

## Explanation

Wait $>3$ min requires arrival in first 2 of the 5 blocking minutes, so $P=2/15$ per vehicle.

$P(\text{at least one}>3)=1-(13/15)^2=56/225\approx\mathbf{0.249\approx0.25}$
