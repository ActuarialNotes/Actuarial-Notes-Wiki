An insurance policy covers losses $L \sim \text{Uniform}[0, 2000]$. The policy pays $(L-500)$ if $500 < L < 1500$ and $1000$ if $L \geq 1500$.

Calculate the expected payment under the policy.

- A) 250
- B) 375
- C) 500
- D) 625
- E) 750

## Explanation

$$E[\text{payment}] = \int_{500}^{1500}\frac{(l-500)}{2000}\,dl + 1000 \cdot \frac{500}{2000}$$

$$= \frac{1}{2000}\left[\frac{(l-500)^2}{2}\right]_{500}^{1500}+\frac{500}{2}$$

$$= \frac{1}{2000}\cdot\frac{1000^2}{2}+250 = \frac{500000}{2000}+250 = 250+250 = \mathbf{500}$$
