Minor accident (prob 0.75): loss $\sim\text{Uniform}[0,b]$. Major (prob 0.25): loss $\sim\text{Uniform}[b,3b]$. Median $=672$. Calculate the mean loss.

- A) 392
- B) 512
- C) 672
- D) 882
- E) 1008

## Explanation

$F(m)=0.75m/b=0.5\Rightarrow m=2b/3=672\Rightarrow b=1008$.

$E[L]=0.75(b/2)+0.25(2b)=3b/8+b/2=7b/8=7\times1008/8=\mathbf{882}$
