You are given the following information about a 20-year bond with face amount 1000:

  i) The bond has an annual coupon rate of $r$ payable semiannually and is redeemable at par.
  ii) The nominal annual yield rate convertible semiannually is 7.2%.
  iii) The amount for accumulation of discount in the seventh coupon payment is 4.36.

Calculate $r$.

- A) 2.1%
- B) 4.0%
- C) 4.3%
- D) 6.0%
- E) 6.9%

## Explanation

Semiannual yield $j = 3.6\%$. For a discount bond ($r < 7.2\%$), accumulation of discount grows.

The book value after the 6th coupon: $1000(r/2)a_{\overline{34}|0.036} + 1000(1.036)^{-34} = 9716.01r + 300.45$ (in units with $r/2$ as semiannual rate).

After the 7th: $9565.79r + 311.26$.

Accumulation of discount at 7th coupon = BV7 $-$ BV6:
$$(9565.79r + 311.26) - (9716.01r + 300.45) = 10.81 - 150.22r = 4.36$$

$$r = (10.81 - 4.36)/150.22 = 0.0429 = 4.3\%$$
