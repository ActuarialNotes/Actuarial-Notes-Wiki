An $n$-year bond with annual coupons has the following characteristics:

  i) The redemption value at maturity is 1890;
  ii) The annual effective yield rate is 6%;
  iii) The book value immediately after the third coupon is 1254.87; and
  iv) The book value immediately after the fourth coupon is 1277.38.

Calculate $n$.

- A) 16
- B) 17
- C) 18
- D) 19
- E) 20

## Explanation

Book values are linked by $BV_3(1+i) - Fr = BV_4$:

$$
\begin{align*}
1254.87(1.06) - Fr &= 1277.38 \\
Fr &= 52.7822
\end{align*}
$$

The prospective formula at time 3:

$$
\begin{align*}
1254.87 &= 52.7822\cdot\frac{1-1.06^{-(n-3)}}{0.06} + 1890 \cdot 1.06^{-(n-3)} \\
375.1667 &= 1010.297 \cdot 1.06^{-(n-3)} \\
n - 3 &= \frac{\ln(375.1667/1010.297)}{-\ln(1.06)} = 17
\end{align*}
$$

Thus $n = 20$.
