[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Close Year**

## Definition
==Close Year== is a data organization method where claims are grouped by the year in which they were closed or settled.

## Uses
- Analyzing claim settlement patterns
- Measuring claims department efficiency
- Understanding closure rates
- Limited use in ratemaking

## Characteristics
- Significant lag from accident date
- Useful for operational metrics
- Not commonly used for rate indications
- Helps assess claim handling speed
