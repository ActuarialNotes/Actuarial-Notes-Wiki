A group of health insurance policyholders is composed of 60% men and 40% women. Of the male policyholders, 20% are smokers. Given that a policyholder from the group smokes, the probability that the policyholder is female is 20%.

Calculate the percentage of female policyholders who are smokers.

- A) 7.50%
- B) 8.00%
- C) 12.00%
- D) 13.33%
- E) 20.00%

## Explanation

$P(\text{smoke}\cap\text{male}) = 0.60 \times 0.20 = 0.12$

Let $P(\text{smoke}\cap\text{female}) = p$. Then $P(\text{smoke}) = 0.12+p$.

$$P(\text{female}\mid\text{smoke}) = \frac{p}{0.12+p} = 0.20$$

$$p = 0.20(0.12+p) = 0.024+0.20p \implies 0.80p = 0.024 \implies p = 0.03$$

$$P(\text{smoke}\mid\text{female}) = \frac{0.03}{0.40} = \mathbf{7.5\%}$$
