# Combinatorics

## Definition

==Combinatorics== is the branch of mathematics concerning counting, arrangement, and selection of objects. The fundamental counting principle states: if task $A$ has $m$ ways and task $B$ has $n$ ways, together there are $m \times n$ ways.

$$ |A \times B| = m \times n $$

> [!example]- <u>Example</u>
> A restaurant offers 4 appetizers, 6 entrees, and 3 desserts. How many different 3-course meals can be ordered?
>
> By the fundamental counting principle:
> $$4 \times 6 \times 3 = 72 \text{ different meals}$$
>
> If a license plate consists of 3 letters followed by 4 digits, the total number of plates is:
> $$26^3 \times 10^4 = 17{,}576 \times 10{,}000 = 175{,}760{,}000$$
