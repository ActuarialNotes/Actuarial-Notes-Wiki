The probability that a phone call produces a completed survey is 0.25. Calculate the probability that more than three phone calls are required to produce one completed survey.

- A) 0.32
- B) 0.42
- C) 0.44
- D) 0.56
- E) 0.58

## Explanation

$P(X>3) = (0.75)^3 = 0.421875 \approx \mathbf{0.42}$
