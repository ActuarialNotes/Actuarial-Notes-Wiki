Fund X receives a deposit of 1000 at time 0. Fund X accumulates at a nominal rate of interest $k$, compounded semiannually.

Fund Y receives a deposit of 921.90 at time 0. Fund Y accumulates at a nominal rate of discount, also equal to $k$, compounded semiannually.

At the end of 5 years, the accumulated amount in Fund X and the accumulated amount in Fund Y are both equal to $P$.

Calculate $P$.

- A) 1820
- B) 1970
- C) 2100
- D) 2240
- E) 2370

## Explanation

Fund X: $P = 1000\left(1+\dfrac{k}{2}\right)^{10}$

Fund Y: $P = 921.90\left(1-\dfrac{k}{2}\right)^{-10}$

Setting equal: $1000\left(1+\dfrac{k}{2}\right)^{10} = 921.90\left(1-\dfrac{k}{2}\right)^{-10}$

$$0.9219 = \left[\left(1+\frac{k}{2}\right)\left(1-\frac{k}{2}\right)\right]^{10} = \left(1-\frac{k^2}{4}\right)^{10}$$

$$1 - \frac{k^2}{4} = 0.9919 \implies k^2 = 0.0324 \implies k = 0.18$$

$$P = 1000\left(1+\frac{0.18}{2}\right)^{10} = 1000(1.09)^{10} = 2367.36$$
