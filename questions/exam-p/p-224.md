Registration time has density symmetric about 6.5, proportional to $1/(t+1)$ for $t\in[0,6.5]$. Find the 60th percentile.

- A) 4.01
- B) 7.80
- C) 8.99
- D) 10.22
- E) 10.51

## Explanation

Normalization: $c=1/(2\ln7.5)$. For $t_0>6.5$: $\frac{1}{2\ln7.5}\ln\!\left(\frac{7.5}{14-t_0}\right)=0.1\Rightarrow14-t_0=7.5^{0.8}\approx5.01\Rightarrow t_0\approx\mathbf{8.99}$
