# FSRA OCF-1 Redesign (May 2025)

## Overview
FSRA released a redesigned Application for Accident Benefits (OCF-1) — a single, consumer-friendlier form — modernizing the gateway document to Ontario's SABS system ahead of, but separate from, the substantive July 2026 benefit reforms.

## Key Details
*   **Plain-Language Overhaul:** Consolidated and simplified the application that injured persons must complete to access accident benefits.
*   **Sequencing Significance:** Demonstrated FSRA's staged forms-modernization program, which subsequently produced the full suite of reform-updated forms (including the OPCF 47R endorsement) for the 2026 transition.

## Multi-Agent Execution Mandates
*   **Claims & IT:** Intake systems, OCR/document automation, and adjuster workflows updated to the new form version.
*   **CX:** Customer-facing claims guidance and FNOL scripts refreshed to match the simplified application.
