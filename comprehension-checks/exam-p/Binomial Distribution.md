Which situation is correctly modeled by a binomial distribution?

- A) The number of heads in 20 independent flips of a fair coin
- B) The number of red cards drawn when dealing 5 cards without replacement
- C) The number of flips until the first head appears
- D) The number of claims arriving over a month at a constant rate

<!-- rationale: 1: hypergeometric (without replacement, dependent) · 2: geometric (trials until first success) · 3: Poisson (rate over an interval) -->
