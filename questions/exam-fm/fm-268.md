An insurance company aims to structure a portfolio of assets and liabilities so that:

  i) Its assets and liabilities have equal present values and equal modified durations.
  ii) The convexity of its assets exceeds the convexity of its liabilities.

Determine which term precisely describes the company's position.

- A) Diversified
- B) Fully immunized
- C) Fully leveraged
- D) Exactly matched
- E) Redington immunized

## Explanation

The three conditions for Redington immunization are exactly:
1. PV of assets = PV of liabilities
2. Duration of assets = Duration of liabilities (equivalently, modified durations are equal)
3. Convexity of assets > Convexity of liabilities

These conditions are precisely what is described in (i) and (ii). This is the definition of **Redington immunization**. (Full immunization additionally requires the asset cash flows to bracket the liability cash flows, which is not stated here.)
