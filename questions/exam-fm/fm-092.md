A loan of $X$ is repaid with level annual payments at the end of each year for 10 years. You are given:

  i) The interest paid in the first year is 3600; and
  ii) The principal repaid in the 6th year is 4871.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 44,000
- B) 45,250
- C) 46,500
- D) 48,000
- E) 50,000

## Explanation

Let $P$ be the annual payment and $v = (1+i)^{-1}$.

From (i): $P(1 - v^{10}) = 3600$, so $P \cdot i \cdot a_{\overline{10}|} = 3600$.

The principal repaid at time $k$ is $Pv^{n-k+1}$. From (ii): $Pv^{10-6+1} = Pv^5 = 4871$.

Dividing: $\dfrac{P(1-v^{10})}{Pv^5} = \dfrac{3600}{4871}$, so $\dfrac{1-v^{10}}{v^5} = 0.739068$.

This gives $v^5 = 0.69656$, $v^{10} = 0.485195$, and $i = 0.485195^{-1/10} - 1 = 0.075$.

$$X = P \cdot a_{\overline{10}|} = \frac{3600}{0.075} = 48,000$$
