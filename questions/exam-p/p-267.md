The lifetime of a certain electronic device has an exponential distribution with a mean of 0.50.

Calculate the probability that the lifetime of the device is greater than 0.70, given that it is greater than 0.40.

- A) 0.203
- B) 0.247
- C) 0.449
- D) 0.549
- E) 0.861

## Explanation

By the **memoryless property** of the exponential:

$$P(T > 0.70 \mid T > 0.40) = P(T > 0.30)$$

With mean $= 0.50$, rate $\lambda = 2$:

$$P(T > 0.30) = e^{-2(0.30)} = e^{-0.6} \approx \mathbf{0.5488 \approx 0.549}$$
