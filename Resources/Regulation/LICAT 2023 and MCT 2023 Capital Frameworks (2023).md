# LICAT 2023 & MCT 2023 (Effective January 1, 2023)

## Overview
Concurrent with IFRS 17 adoption, OSFI implemented fully revised capital guidelines — LICAT 2023 for life insurers and MCT 2023 for P&C insurers — rewiring available and required capital definitions around IFRS 17 balance sheet constructs such as the contractual service margin.

## Key Details
*   **CSM Treatment:** Portions of the CSM recognized within available capital under LICAT, a decisive calibration issue for life insurers' reported ratios.
*   **Policy Neutrality Goal:** OSFI calibrated the frameworks to keep aggregate industry capital broadly stable through the accounting transition rather than re-setting capital levels.
*   **Return Rewrite:** All regulatory returns (LIFE, P&C) and internal capital forecasting models rebuilt on IFRS 17 data architecture.

## Multi-Agent Execution Mandates
*   **Actuarial & Finance:** Capital ratios became jointly dependent on actuarial measurement choices (cohorts, risk adjustment, discounting), binding capital management to IFRS 17 methodology governance.
*   **Risk:** ORSA and internal targets recalibrated to the new ratio mechanics; stress testing rebuilt on IFRS 17 sensitivities.
*   **IT:** Capital engines integrated with the new sub-ledgers; superseded by subsequent annual guideline updates (e.g., MCT 2026).
