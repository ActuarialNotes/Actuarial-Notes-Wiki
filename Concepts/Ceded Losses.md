[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Ceded Losses**

## Definition
==Ceded Losses== are the portion of losses transferred to reinsurers under reinsurance agreements.

## Formula
```
Ceded = Gross × Cession %

Or for excess:
Ceded = MAX(0, Gross - Retention)
```
