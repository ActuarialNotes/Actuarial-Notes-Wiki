You are given the following stock prices of company CAS:

| Day | Stock Price |
|---|---|
| 1 | 538 |
| 2 | 548 |
| 3 | 528 |
| 4 | 608 |
| 5 | 598 |
| 6 | 589 |
| 7 | 548 |
| 8 | 514 |
| 9 | 501 |
| 10 | 498 |

- The average stock price in those days is 547

Calculate the sample autocovariance at lag 3.

- A) Less than -500
- B) At least -500, but less than -250
- C) At least -250, but less than 0
- D) At least 0, but less than 250
- E) At least 250

## Explanation

The sample autocovariance at lag $k$ is

$$\hat\gamma_k = \frac{1}{n}\sum_{t=1}^{n-k}(x_t - \bar{x})(x_{t+k} - \bar{x}).$$

The deviations $d_t = x_t - 547$ are

$$-9,\ 1,\ -19,\ 61,\ 51,\ 42,\ 1,\ -33,\ -46,\ -49.$$

Lag-3 cross products $d_t \cdot d_{t+3}$:

$$(-9)(61) + (1)(51) + (-19)(42) + (61)(1) + (51)(-33) + (42)(-46) + (1)(-49)$$
$$= -549 + 51 - 798 + 61 - 1683 - 1932 - 49 = -4899.$$

$$\hat\gamma_3 = \frac{-4899}{10} = -489.9.$$

This falls in $[-500, -250)$, so the answer is **B**.
