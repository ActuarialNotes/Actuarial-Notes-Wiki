Losses $\sim\text{Uniform}[0,450]$, deductible $d$. Expected unreimbursed portion $=56$. Calculate $d$.

- A) 60
- B) 87
- C) 112
- D) 169
- E) 224

## Explanation

$E[\min(X,d)]=d-d^2/900=56\Rightarrow d^2-900d+50400=0\Rightarrow d=\mathbf{60}$ (verified: $60-3600/900=56$)
