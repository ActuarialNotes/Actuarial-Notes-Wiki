The probability that an insured will file exactly one claim in a given year is 0.10; the probability of no claims is 0.90. Claim outcomes in different years are independent. Calculate the probability that an insured will file at least one claim over the next three years.

- A) 0.10
- B) 0.27
- C) 0.30
- D) 0.50
- E) 0.73

## Explanation

$$
\begin{align*}
P(\text{at least one claim in 3 years}) &= 1-P(\text{no claims in any year}) \\
                                        &= 1-(0.90)^3 \\
                                        &= 1-0.729 \\
                                        &= \mathbf{0.271 \approx 0.27}
\end{align*}
$$
