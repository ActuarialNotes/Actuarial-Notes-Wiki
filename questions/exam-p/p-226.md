$X\sim\text{Exp}(\text{mean }10)$, deductible $d>0$. $Y=(X-d)_+$. Calculate $\text{Var}(Y)$.

- A) $100-d$
- B) $(10-d)^2$
- C) $100e^{-d/10}$
- D) $100\!\left(2e^{-d/10}-e^{-d/5}\right)$
- E) $(10-d)^2\!\left(2e^{-d/10}-e^{-d/5}\right)$

## Explanation

By memoryless property: $E[Y]=10e^{-d/10}$, $E[Y^2]=200e^{-d/10}$.

$$\text{Var}(Y)=200e^{-d/10}-(10e^{-d/10})^2=\mathbf{100(2e^{-d/10}-e^{-d/5})}$$
