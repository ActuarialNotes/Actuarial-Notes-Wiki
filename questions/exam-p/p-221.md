20% chance of no loss (claim=0). If loss: claim $\sim N(1000,400^2)$. Calculate the median claim amount.

- A) 663
- B) 790
- C) 873
- D) 994
- E) 1000

## Explanation

$0.20+0.80\Phi\left(\frac{m-1000}{400}\right)=0.5\Rightarrow\Phi(z)=0.375\Rightarrow z\approx-0.319$

$m=1000+400(-0.319)\approx\mathbf{873}$
