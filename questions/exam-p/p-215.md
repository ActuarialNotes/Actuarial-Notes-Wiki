Losses are exponentially distributed with mean 4. Deductible is 1. Calculate the median amount the insurer pays.

- A) 1.77
- B) 2.08
- C) 2.12
- D) 2.77
- E) 3.12

## Explanation

$P(Y\leq m)=P(X\leq m+1)=0.5\Rightarrow m=4\ln2-1\approx2.773-1=\mathbf{1.77}$
