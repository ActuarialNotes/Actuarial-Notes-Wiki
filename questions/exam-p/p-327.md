Individual burglary claim amounts covered by an insurance company are normally distributed with mean 2500 and standard deviation 500.

The probability that the mean of a random sample of 100 claims will exceed $K$ is 0.01.

Calculate $K$.

- A) 2505
- B) 2512
- C) 2616
- D) 3663
- E) 4950

## Explanation

$\bar{X} \sim N(2500, 500^2/100) = N(2500, 2500)$, $\text{SD}(\bar{X})=50$.

$P(\bar{X}>K) = 0.01$:

$$K = 2500+50\times\Phi^{-1}(0.99) = 2500+50\times 2.326 = 2500+116.3 \approx \mathbf{2616}$$
