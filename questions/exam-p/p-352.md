$X$, $Y$, and $Z$ are pairwise uncorrelated random variables. $\text{Var}(X)=1$, $\text{Var}(Y)=2$, $\text{Var}(Z)=3$.

Calculate $\text{Cov}(X+Y,\; X+Z)$.

- A) -1
- B) 0
- C) 1
- D) 3
- E) 6

## Explanation

$$\text{Cov}(X+Y,\; X+Z)$$

$$= \text{Cov}(X,X)+\text{Cov}(X,Z)+\text{Cov}(Y,X)+\text{Cov}(Y,Z)$$

$$= \text{Var}(X)+0+0+0 = \mathbf{1}$$
