# Venn Diagram

## Definition

A ==Venn diagram== is a diagram showing all possible logical relations between a finite collection of sets using overlapping circles.

> [!example]- <u>Example</u>
> Suppose in a class of 30 students, 18 study math, 15 study science, and 10 study both. A Venn diagram shows:
> - Math only: $18 - 10 = 8$
> - Science only: $15 - 10 = 5$
> - Both: $10$
> - Neither: $30 - 8 - 5 - 10 = 7$
>
> The two overlapping circles represent math and science, with the intersection region containing the 10 students who study both.
