Dental expense $\sim\text{Uniform}[200,1200]$. Primary plan covers up to 400; supplemental covers up to 500 of remaining. Let $Y$ = supplemental benefit. Calculate $\text{Var}(Y)$.

- A) 20,833
- B) 26,042
- C) 41,042
- D) 53,333
- E) 83,333

## Explanation

$Y=0$ if $X\leq400$; $Y=X-400$ if $400<X\leq900$; $Y=500$ if $X>900$.

$E[Y]=125+150=275$, $E[Y^2]=41667+75000=116667$.

$\text{Var}(Y)=116667-275^2=\mathbf{41042}$
