A 20-year bond with 8% annual coupons is purchased at a price of $P$. The face amount of the bond is $F$. The price assumes an annual effective yield rate of 4%.

The change in book value of the bond during the 8th year is equal to 43.24.

Calculate $P$.

- A) 2315
- B) 2470
- C) 2625
- D) 2780
- E) 3085

## Explanation

The change in book value during year 8 equals the amortization of premium in that coupon. Since the coupon 8% > yield 4%, bond is at a premium.

Amortization of premium at coupon $k$: $(Fr-Ci)v^{n-k+1} = (0.08F - 0.04F)v^{n-k+1} = 0.04F \cdot v^{13}$

$$\begin{align*}
43.24 &= 0.04F \cdot (1.04)^{-13} = 0.04F(0.60057) \\
F &= \frac{43.24}{0.02402} = 1800
\end{align*}$$

$$\begin{align*}
P &= 0.08(1800)\,a_{\overline{20}|0.04} + 1800(1.04)^{-20} \\
&= 144(13.5903) + 1800(0.45639) \\
&= 1956.98 + 821.50 \\
&= 2778.50
\end{align*}$$
