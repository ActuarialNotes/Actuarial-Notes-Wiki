A fair die is rolled repeatedly. Let the random variable $X$ be the total number of rolls required to obtain three sixes.

Calculate $\text{Var}(X)$.

- A) 5/12
- B) 18/25
- C) 15
- D) 30
- E) 90

## Explanation

$X \sim \text{Negative Binomial}(r=3,\; p=1/6)$.

$$\text{Var}(X) = \frac{r(1-p)}{p^2} = \frac{3 \times (5/6)}{(1/6)^2}$$

$$= \frac{3 \times 5/6}{1/36} = \frac{5/2}{1/36} = 5/2 \times 36 = \mathbf{90}$$
