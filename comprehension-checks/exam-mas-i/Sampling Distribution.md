Claims have population standard deviation σ = 400. For random samples of size n = 64, the standard deviation of the sample mean x̄ (its standard error) is:

- A) 50 = 400 / √64
- B) 400 — x̄ has the same spread as an individual claim
- C) 6.25 = 400 / 64
- D) 3,200 = 400 × √64
