A loan of 1500 is to be repaid with payments made at the end of each year for 20 years. There are two repayment options:

  i) Equal payments at an annual effective interest rate of 5%.
  ii) Non-level payments of interest on the unpaid balance at an annual effective interest rate of $i$, plus 75 of principal.

The sum of the payments is the same under the two options.

Calculate $i$.

- A) 5.26%
- B) 5.51%
- C) 5.76%
- D) 6.01%
- E) 6.26%

## Explanation

Option 1: $P = 1500/a_{\overline{20}|0.05} = 120.36$. Total $= 20(120.36) = 2407.28$.

Option 2: total principal $= 20(75) = 1500$. Total interest: year $k$ balance $= 1500 - 75(k-1)$, so interest $= i(1500-75(k-1))$.

Total interest $= i\sum_{k=1}^{20}(1500-75(k-1)) = i \times 75 \times \sum_{k=1}^{20}(20-k+1) = i \times 75 \times \frac{20(21)}{2}/... $

$= i[1500(20)-75(0+1+\cdots+19)] = i[30{,}000 - 75(190)] = i[30{,}000-14{,}250] = 15{,}750i$

Total payments $= 1500 + 15{,}750i = 2407.28 \implies i = 907.28/15{,}750 = 5.76\%$
