Two independent losses $X_1,X_2\sim\text{Exp}(1)$. Calculate $E[\min(X_1,X_2)]$.

- A) 0.25
- B) 0.50
- C) 0.75
- D) 1.00
- E) 1.50

## Explanation

The minimum of $n$ independent $\text{Exp}(\lambda)$ random variables $\sim\text{Exp}(n\lambda)$.

$$E[\min(X_1,X_2)]=\frac{1}{n\lambda}=\frac{1}{2\times1}=\mathbf{0.50}$$
