At an annual effective interest rate of 6%, the present value of a perpetuity immediate with successive annual payments of 6, 8, 10, 12, ..., is equal to $X$.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 100
- B) 656
- C) 695
- D) 1767
- E) 2222

## Explanation

The payments form an arithmetic sequence starting at 6 with common difference 2: $a_k = 4 + 2k$ for $k = 1, 2, 3, \ldots$

$$\begin{align*}
X &= \sum_{k=1}^{\infty}(4+2k)v^k \\
&= 4a_{\overline{\infty}|0.06} + 2(Ia)_{\overline{\infty}|0.06} \\
&= \frac{4}{0.06} + 2\cdot\frac{1.06}{0.06^2} \\
&= 66.67 + 588.89 \\
&= 655.56 \approx 656
\end{align*}$$
