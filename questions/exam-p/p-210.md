$E$ and $F$ are independent with $P[E]=0.84$ and $P[F]=0.65$. Calculate the probability that exactly one of the two events occurs.

- A) 0.056
- B) 0.398
- C) 0.546
- D) 0.650
- E) 0.944

## Explanation

$0.84(0.35)+0.16(0.65)=0.294+0.104=\mathbf{0.398}$
