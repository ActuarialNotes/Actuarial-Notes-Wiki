Jia Wen has a liability of 12,000 due in eight years. This liability will be met with payments of 5000 in five years and $B$ in $8 + b$ years. Jia Wen is employing a full immunization strategy using an annual effective interest rate of 3%.

Calculate $\dfrac{B}{b}$.

- A) 2807
- B) 2873
- C) 2902
- D) 2976
- E) 3019

## Explanation

Full immunization conditions:

**PV match:**
$$5000(1.03)^3 + B(1.03)^{-b} = 12{,}000$$
$$5463.635 + B(1.03)^{-b} = 12{,}000 \implies B(1.03)^{-b} = 6536.365$$

**Duration match:**
$$3(5000)(1.03)^3 - bB(1.03)^{-b} = 0$$
$$16{,}390.905 - b(6536.365) = 0 \implies b = 2.5076$$

$$B = 6536.365(1.03)^{2.5076} = 7039.27$$

$$\frac{B}{b} = \frac{7039.27}{2.5076} = 2807.12$$
