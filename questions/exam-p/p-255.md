A dental insurance company pays 100% of the cost of fillings (50 each) and 70% of the cost of root canals (500 each). The number of annual fillings has probabilities: 0 (0.60), 1 (0.20), 2 (0.15), 3 (0.05). The number of annual root canals has probabilities: 0 (0.80), 1 (0.20). Calculate the expected annual payment per policyholder.

- A) 90.00
- B) 102.50
- C) 132.50
- D) 250.00
- E) 400.00

## Explanation

$E[\text{fillings}] = 0(0.60)+1(0.20)+2(0.15)+3(0.05) = 0.65$

$E[\text{root canals}] = 0(0.80)+1(0.20) = 0.20$

$$E[\text{payment}] = 1.00 \times 50 \times 0.65 + 0.70 \times 500 \times 0.20 = 32.50 + 70.00 = \mathbf{102.50}$$
