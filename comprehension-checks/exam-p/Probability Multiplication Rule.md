A portfolio has 10 policies, 4 of which will claim. Two are drawn at random without replacement. What is the probability both claim?

- A) 4/10 × 3/9 = 0.133
- B) 4/10 × 4/10 = 0.16
- C) 4/10 + 3/9 = 0.733
- D) 4/10 = 0.40
