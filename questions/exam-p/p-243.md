Policy pays $50\max(X,Y)$ up to 100, where $X,Y\sim\text{Discrete Uniform}\{0,1,2,3,4\}$, independent. Calculate expected monthly benefit.

- A) 70
- B) 90
- C) 92
- D) 95
- E) 140

## Explanation

$P(M=k)=(2k+1)/25$ for $M=\max(X,Y)$. Payment: 0,50,100,100,100 for $M=0,1,2,3,4$.

$E[W]=0(1/25)+50(3/25)+100(5+7+9)/25=150/25+2100/25=2250/25=\mathbf{90}$
