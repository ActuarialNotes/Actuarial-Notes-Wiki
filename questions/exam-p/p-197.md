A drawer contains four pairs of socks, with each pair a different color. One sock at a time is randomly drawn from the drawer until a matching pair is obtained.

Calculate the probability that the maximum number of draws is required.

- A) 0.0006
- B) 0.0095
- C) 0.0417
- D) 0.1429
- E) 0.2286

## Explanation

The maximum draws needed is 5 (draw 4 different colors, then the 5th must match). This requires the first four draws to all be different colors.

Draw 1: any sock ($8/8 = 1$).
Draw 2: different color from draw 1: $6/7$.
Draw 3: different from first two colors: $4/6$.
Draw 4: different from first three colors: $2/5$.

$$
\begin{align*}
P(\text{max draws}) &= 1 \times \frac{6}{7} \times \frac{4}{6} \times \frac{2}{5} \\
                    &= \frac{48}{210} \\
                    &= \frac{8}{35} \\
                    &\approx \mathbf{0.2286}
\end{align*}
$$
