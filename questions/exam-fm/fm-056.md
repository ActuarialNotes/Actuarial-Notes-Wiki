Aakash has a liability of 6000 due in four years. This liability will be met with payments of $A$ in two years and $B$ in six years. Aakash is employing a full immunization strategy using an annual effective interest rate of 5%.

Calculate $|A - B|$.

- A) 0
- B) 146
- C) 293
- D) 586
- E) 881

## Explanation

Full immunization requires two conditions (present value matching and duration matching):

**PV match:**
$$A(1.05)^2 + B(1.05)^{-2} = 6000$$

**Duration match** (convexity condition — equate weighted times):
$$2A(1.05)^2 - 2B(1.05)^{-2} = 0 \cdot \text{...wait, use derivative condition}$$

The duration condition equates the "dollar duration" sensitivity:
$$2A(1.05)^1 - 2B(1.05)^{-3} = 0$$

Solving simultaneously:
$$A = 2721.09,\quad B = 3307.50$$

$$|A - B| = 586.41$$
