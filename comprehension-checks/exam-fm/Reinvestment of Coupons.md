A bond is bought to yield 7%, but its coupons can only be reinvested at 4%. The investor's realized annual return over the holding period will be:

- A) Below 7%, because coupons grow more slowly than assumed
- B) Exactly 7%, the purchase yield
- C) Above 7%, since extra interest accumulates
- D) Exactly 4%, the reinvestment rate

<!-- rationale: 1: ignores reinvestment risk · 2: reverses the effect · 3: overcorrects all the way to the reinvestment rate -->
