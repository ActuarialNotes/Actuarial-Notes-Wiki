[[Actuarial Notes Wiki|Wiki]] / [[Exam 5 (CAS)]] / **Reserving Data Organization**

## Definition
==Reserving Data Organization== is the systematic arrangement of loss and exposure data by time periods to facilitate accurate reserve analysis and projection of ultimate losses.

## Primary Methods
- Accident year (most common)
- Policy year
- Underwriting year
- Report year
