The joint distribution of auto claims $X$ and home claims $Y$ is given by:

| $X \backslash Y$ | 0 | 1 |
|---|---|---|
| 0 | 0.64 | 0.16 |
| 1 | 0.16 | 0.04 |

Calculate $\text{Corr}(X, Y)$.

- A) -0.50
- B) -0.25
- C) 0.00
- D) 0.25
- E) 0.50

## Explanation

$P(X=1)=0.20$, $P(Y=1)=0.20$, $P(X=1,Y=1)=0.04$.

$E[XY]=P(X=1,Y=1)=0.04$ and $E[X]E[Y]=(0.20)(0.20)=0.04$.

$$\text{Cov}(X,Y) = 0.04-0.04 = 0$$

Note: $0.64 = 0.80 \times 0.80$, confirming $X$ and $Y$ are **independent**, so:

$$\text{Corr}(X,Y) = \mathbf{0}$$
