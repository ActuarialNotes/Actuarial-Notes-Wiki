## Definition
==Schedule Rating== is a ratemaking technique allowing underwriters to apply discretionary debits or credits based on specific risk characteristics not captured in the base classification.

## Factors
- Safety programs
- Management quality
- Loss control measures
- Physical hazards
- Moral hazards

## Constraints
Usually subject to:
- Maximum debit/credit limits
- Documentation requirements
- Regulatory approval
- Consistency standards
