A debt is amortized with 120 level payments at the end of each month based on an annual effective interest rate of 8%. The amount of principal in the sixth payment is 600.

Calculate the amount of principal in the 24th payment.

- A) 532
- B) 673
- C) 700
- D) 704
- E) 784

## Explanation

Monthly rate $j = 1.08^{1/12} - 1 = 0.006434$.

The principal repaid at payment $k$ is $P_k = R \cdot v^{n-k+1}$, which grows by a factor of $(1+j)$ from one payment to the next. Therefore:

$$P_{24} = P_6 \cdot (1+j)^{24-6} = 600(1.006434)^{18} = 600(1.12247) = 673.48 \approx 673$$
