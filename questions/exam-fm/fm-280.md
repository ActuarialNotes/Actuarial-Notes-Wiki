At the beginning of each year, a payment of 5000 is invested in a fund. The payments earn an annual effective interest rate of 8%. At the end of each year, the interest is reinvested in the fund at an annual effective interest rate of 5%. The amount in the fund at the end of ten years, immediately prior to the 11th annual payment, is $X$.

Calculate $X$ to the nearest 100.

- A) 67,600
- B) 70,300
- C) 75,700
- D) 78,200
- E) 80,700

## Explanation

Each of the 10 deposits of 5000 earns 8% each year, but the interest is reinvested at 5%.

Accumulated value at end of year 10:
$$X = 5000(10) + 5000(0.08)(Is)_{\overline{10}|0.05}$$

$$= 50{,}000 + 400 \cdot \frac{\ddot{s}_{\overline{10}|0.05}-10}{0.05}$$

$$
\begin{align*}
50{,}000 + 400 \cdot \frac{13.2068-10}{0.05} &= 50{,}000 + 400(64.135) \\
                                             &= 50{,}000 + 25{,}654 \\
                                             &= 75{,}654 \approx 75{,}700
\end{align*}
$$
