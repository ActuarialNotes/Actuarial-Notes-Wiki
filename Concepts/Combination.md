# Combination

## Definition

A ==combination== is a selection of $r$ objects from $n$ without regard to order.

$$ \binom{n}{r} = \frac{n!}{r!(n-r)!} $$

> [!example]- <u>Example</u>
> A committee of 3 people is to be chosen from a group of 10. How many ways can this be done?
>
> $$\binom{10}{3} = \frac{10!}{3! \cdot 7!} = \frac{10 \times 9 \times 8}{3 \times 2 \times 1} = 120$$
>
> From a standard 52-card deck, the number of 5-card poker hands is:
> $$\binom{52}{5} = \frac{52!}{5! \cdot 47!} = 2{,}598{,}960$$
