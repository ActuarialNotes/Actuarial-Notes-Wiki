$X\sim\text{Poisson}(1.70)$, $Y\sim\text{Poisson}(2.30)$, independent. Calculate $\text{Var}(X-Y\mid X+Y=3)$.

- A) 1.71
- B) 1.77
- C) 2.93
- D) 3.14
- E) 4.00

## Explanation

Given $X+Y=3$: $X|X+Y=3\sim\text{Binomial}(3, p)$ where $p=1.7/4.0=0.425$.

$\text{Var}(X-Y|X+Y=3)=\text{Var}(2X-3|X+Y=3)=4\text{Var}(X|X+Y=3)$

$=4\times3\times0.425\times0.575=\mathbf{2.9325\approx2.93}$
