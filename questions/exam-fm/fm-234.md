A bank issues two 20-year par-value bonds providing annual coupons. Each bond sells for the same price and provides an annual effective yield rate of 6.5%.

The first bond has a redemption value of 6000 and a coupon of 7.6% paid annually. The second bond has a redemption value of 7500 and a coupon of $r$% paid annually.

Calculate $r$.

- A) 5.6
- B) 5.9
- C) 6.1
- D) 6.7
- E) 7.2

## Explanation

Bond 1 price: $P = 0.076(6000)a_{\overline{20}|0.065} + 6000v^{20} = 456a_{\overline{20}|} + 6000v^{20}$

$= 456(11.0185) + 6000(0.28380) = 5024.44 + 1702.80 = 6727.24$

Bond 2 price $= 6727.24$:
$$6727.24 = r(7500/100)(7500)a_{\overline{20}|0.065} + 7500v^{20}$$

Wait, coupon $= r\%(7500)$:
$$6727.24 = (r/100)(7500)(11.0185) + 7500(0.28380) = 826.39r + 2128.50$$

$$r = (6727.24 - 2128.50)/826.39 = 4598.74/826.39 = 5.564 \approx 5.6$$
