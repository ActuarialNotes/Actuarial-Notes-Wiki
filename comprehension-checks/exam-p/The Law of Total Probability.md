A portfolio is 30% young (claim rate 0.20), 50% middle-aged (0.10), and 20% senior (0.15). What is the overall probability of a claim?

- A) 0.140
- B) 0.150
- C) 0.450
- D) 0.075

<!-- rationale: 1: simple average of the rates, ignoring class sizes · 2: sums the rates without weighting · 3: multiplies all three rates -->
