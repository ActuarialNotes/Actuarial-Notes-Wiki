A loan of 1500 is to be repaid with payments made at the end of each year for 20 years. There are two repayment options:

  i) Equal payments at an annual effective interest rate of 5%.
  ii) Non-level payments of interest on the unpaid balance at an annual effective interest rate of $i$, plus 75 of principal.

The sum of the payments is the same under the two options.

Calculate $i$.

- A) 5.26%
- B) 5.51%
- C) 5.76%
- D) 6.01%
- E) 6.26%

## Explanation

Option 1:

$$
\begin{align*}
P &= 1500/a_{\overline{20}|0.05} \\
  &= 120.36 \\
\text{Total} &= 20(120.36) \\
             &= 2407.28
\end{align*}
$$

Option 2: total principal $= 20(75) = 1500$. The balance in year $k$ is $1500 - 75(k-1)$, so total interest is:

$$
\begin{align*}
i\sum_{k=1}^{20}[1500-75(k-1)] &= i\bigl[30{,}000 - 75(190)\bigr] \\
                               &= 15{,}750\,i
\end{align*}
$$

Setting total payments equal:

$$
\begin{align*}
1500 + 15{,}750i &= 2407.28 \\
i &= \frac{907.28}{15{,}750} \\
  &= 5.76\%
\end{align*}
$$
