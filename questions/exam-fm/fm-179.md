An investment of 10,000 produces a series of 30 annual payments. The first payment of $X$ is made one year after the investment is made. Each successive payment decreases by 5 from the previous payment.

At an annual effective interest rate of 5%, calculate $X$.

- A) 685
- B) 695
- C) 705
- D) 715
- E) 725

## Explanation

The payments form a decreasing arithmetic annuity: $(X), (X-5), (X-10), \ldots$

$$10{,}000 = (X+5)a_{\overline{30}|0.05} - 5(Ia)_{\overline{30}|0.05}$$

$$= (X+5)(15.37245) - 5\cdot\frac{\ddot{a}_{\overline{30}|} - 30v^{30}}{0.05}$$

$$= (X+5)(15.37245) - 5\cdot\frac{16.14107 - 6.94132}{0.05}$$

$$= 15.37245X + 76.862 - 919.975 = 15.37245X - 843.113$$

$$X = (10{,}000 + 843.113)/15.37245 = 705.36$$
