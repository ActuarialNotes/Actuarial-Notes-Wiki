A 20-year loan of 500 based on a 5% annual effective interest rate is repaid with level installments at the end of each year.

Determine the installment in which the principal and interest portions are most nearly equal to each other.

- A) 1st
- B) 7th
- C) 10th
- D) 13th
- E) 20th

## Explanation

Principal portion at payment $t$: $P_t = Rv^{n-t+1}$. Interest portion: $I_t = R(1-v^{n-t+1})$.

They are equal when $v^{n-t+1} = 1 - v^{n-t+1}$, i.e., $2v^{21-t} = 1$, so $v^{21-t} = 0.5$.

$(21-t)\ln(v) = \ln(0.5) \implies (21-t)(-\ln(1.05)) = -\ln 2$

$21-t = \ln 2 / \ln(1.05) = 0.6931/0.04879 = 14.2$

$t = 21 - 14.2 = 6.8 \approx 7$th payment.
