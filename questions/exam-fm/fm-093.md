An investor purchased a 25-year bond with semiannual coupons, redeemable at par, for a price of 10,000. The annual effective yield rate is 7.05%, and the annual coupon rate is 7%.

Calculate the redemption value of the bond.

- A) 9,918
- B) 9,942
- C) 9,981
- D) 10,059
- E) 10,083

## Explanation

Let $j$ = periodic (semiannual) yield rate, $r$ = periodic coupon rate, $F$ = redemption value, $n = 50$ periods.

$$j = (1.0705)^{1/2} - 1 = 0.03465, \quad r = 0.035$$

The bond price equation $P = Fv_j^n + Fra_{\overline{n}|j}$ solved for $F$:

$$F = \frac{P}{v_j^n + r \cdot a_{\overline{n}|j}} = \frac{10,000}{(1.03465)^{-50} + 0.035 \cdot a_{\overline{50}|0.03465}} = \frac{10,000}{0.18211 + 0.035(23.6044)} = 9,918$$
