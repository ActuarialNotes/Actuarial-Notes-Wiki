A claim amount $X$ has a mixed distribution: $P[X=0] = 0.60$, and given $X > 0$, $X$ is exponentially distributed with mean 1.

Calculate the median of $X$.

- A) 0
- B) 0.40
- C) 0.60
- D) 0.80
- E) 1.00

## Explanation

$P(X = 0) = 0.60 > 0.50$.

Since the probability mass at zero exceeds 0.50, the median is the smallest value $m$ such that $P(X \leq m) \geq 0.50$.

$$P(X \leq 0) = 0.60 \geq 0.50$$

Therefore the median is $\mathbf{0}$.
