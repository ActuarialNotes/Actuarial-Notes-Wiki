An investor will accumulate 10,000 at the end of ten years by making level deposits of $X$ at the beginning of each year. The deposits earn 12% simple interest at the end of every year but the interest is reinvested at an annual effective rate of 8%.

Calculate $X$.

- A) 508.79
- B) 541.47
- C) 569.84
- D) 597.73
- E) 608.42

## Explanation

Each deposit of $X$ earns simple interest of $0.12X$ at the end of each subsequent year, and those interest payments are reinvested at 8%.

The deposit made at the beginning of year $k$ (time $k-1$) earns $0.12X$ at times $k, k+1, \ldots, 10$. The deposit itself is returned at time 10.

Total accumulated at time 10:
$$10X + 0.12X\left[(Is)_{\overline{10}|0.08}\right] = 10X + 0.12X\cdot\frac{\ddot{s}_{\overline{10}|0.08}-10}{0.08}$$
$$= 10X + 0.12X\cdot\frac{15.6455-10}{0.08} = 10X + 0.12X(70.5688) = 18.4683X$$

$$10,000 = 18.4683X \implies X = 541.47$$
